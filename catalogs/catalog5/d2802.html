THIRD GENERATION

9. Maria Theresia Vanzieleghem was born on 14 Mar 1876 in Lichtervelde. She died on 18 Jul 1951 in Meulebeke.

She was married to Cyriel De Backer +-1911. Cyriel De Backer died in Meulebeke.

She was married to Petrus Josephus Joye (son of Bruno Joye and Juliana Cappelle) +-1905. Petrus Josephus Joye was born on 17 Aug 1875 in Passendale. He died +-1909. Maria Theresia Vanzieleghem and Petrus Josephus Joye had the following children:

+14 i. Odiel Michiel Joye.

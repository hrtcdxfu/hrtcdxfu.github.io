SIXTH GENERATION

108. Isidoor Vansieleghem was born on 30 Oct 1848 in Zwevezele. He died on 23 Dec 1934 in Wingene.

He was married to Nathalie Anseeuw on 24 Apr 1878 in Wingene. Nathalie Anseeuw was born on 18 Sep 1850 in Wingene. She died on 28 Aug 1928 in Wingene. Isidoor Vansieleghem and Nathalie Anseeuw had the following children:

+122 i. Maria Vansieleghem.
+123 ii. Elodie Maria Vanzieleghem.
124 iii. Jules Vanzieleghem was born in 1885 in Wingene. emigrated to USA in 1906
125 iv. Irma Vanzieleghem was born in 1887 in Wingene.
126 v. Emelie Vanzieleghem was born on 11 May 1888 in Wingene. She died on 29 May 1977 in Ieper. She was a kloosterzuster scholastica.
127 vi. Zoë Vanzieleghem was born on 6 Jul 1892 in Wingene. She died on 13 Nov 1961 in Gent. She was a kloosterzuster Maria Imelda.
+128 vii. Kamiel Henri Vanzieleghem.

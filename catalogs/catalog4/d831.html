SIXTH GENERATION

92. Franciscus Slabbinck was born on 30 Dec 1808 in Ruddervoorde. He died on 27 Jul 1893 in Ruddervoorde.

He was married to Francisca Van Becelaere on 17 Jul 1840 in Zedelgem. Francisca Van Becelaere was born on 27 Dec 1815 in Zedelgem. She died on 13 Apr 1871 in Ruddervoorde.

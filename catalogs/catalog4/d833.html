SIXTH GENERATION

94. Juliana Slabbinck was born on 6 Feb 1813 in Ruddervoorde. She died on 13 Jan 1894 in Ruddervoorde.

She was married to Jan Wijseure on 27 Sep 1848 in Ruddervoorde. Jan Wijseure was born on 14 Nov 1813 in Ruddervoorde. He died on 15 Apr 1869 in Ruddervoorde.

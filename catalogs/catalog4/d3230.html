FOURTH GENERATION

20. Petrus Van Zieleghem was born on 31 Jan 1770 in Zwevezele. He died on 23 Mar 1838 in Zwevezele.

He was married to Barbara Coussée on 24 Sep 1806 in Zwevezele. Barbara Coussée was born in 1781 in Lichtervelde. She died on 30 Nov 1847 in Zwevezele. Petrus Van Zieleghem and Barbara Coussée had the following children:

66 iii. Jacques Vanzieleghem was born on 2 Nov 1810 in Zwevezele. He died on 8 Dec 1890 in Zwevezele.

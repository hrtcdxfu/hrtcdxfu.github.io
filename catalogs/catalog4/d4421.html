SIXTH GENERATION

98. Anna Maria Blomme was born on 18 Feb 1811 in Ruddervoorde. She died on 8 Dec 1884 in Zedelgem.

She was married to Augustinus Van Steelandt on 1 Feb 1837 in Zwevezele. Augustinus Van Steelandt was born on 10 Jun 1803 in Zwevezele. He died on 24 Dec 1881 in Zedelgem.

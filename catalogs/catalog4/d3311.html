FOURTH GENERATION

18. Maria Anna Van Zieleghem was born on 26 Mar 1768 in Zwevezele. She died on 1 May 1847 in Zwevezele.

She was married to David Vantournout on 14 Sep 1790 in Zwevezele. David Vantournout was born on 23 Mar 1739/40 in Wingene. He died on 20 Sep 1800 in Zwevezele. Maria Anna Van Zieleghem and David Vantournout had the following children:

59 i. Amandus Vantournout was born on 4 Feb 1791 in Wingene.
60 ii. Barbara Vantournout was born on 8 Aug 1792 in Zwevezele. She died on 24 Mar 1845 in Zwevezele.
61 iii. Anna Vantournout was born on 16 May 1795 in Wingene.
62 iv. Joannes Vantournout was born on 22 Jan 1797 in Wingene.
63 v. Franciscus Vantournout was born on 10 May 1799 in Wingene.

ELEVENTH GENERATION

1024. Josephus Van Sieleghem was born on 26 Jan 1638 in Lichtervelde. He died on 14 May 1692 in Lichtervelde. He was RA Brugge archief Lichtervelde reg; nr.353 on 15 Nov 1710 in Lichtervelde. He was married to Maria Lansens on 12 Aug 1657 in Lichtervelde.

ii. Josephus Vansieleghem was born on 28 Sep 1658 in Lichtervelde. He died on 3 Feb 1712 in Ardooie.
iii. Adrianus Vansieleghem was born on 4 May 1661 in Lichtervelde. He died on 3 Feb 1712 in Ardooie.
iv. Pieter Vansieleghem was born on 10 Jan 1664 in Lichtervelde.
v. Clara Vansieleghem was born on 19 Nov 1666 in Lichtervelde. She died on 18 Oct 1747 in Torhout.
vi. Guillielmus Vansieleghem was born on 30 Dec 1668 in Lichtervelde.
vii. Joosijne Vansieleghem was born on 9 Feb 1672 in Lichtervelde. She died on 1 Jul 1739 in Zedelgem.

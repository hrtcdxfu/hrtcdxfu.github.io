FIFTH GENERATION

16. Carolus Ludovicus Vansieleghem was born on 8 Mar 1828 in Leffinge. He died on 14 Aug 1901 in Zandvoorde. He was mijn betovergroorvader. He was married to Paulina Bertina Keters on 7 Sep 1862 in Stene.

17. Paulina Bertina Keters was born on 29 Sep 1834 in Zandvoorde. She died on 2 Sep 1909 in Zandvoorde. Children were:

i. Carolus Franciscus Vansieleghem was born on 22 Jan 1863 in Bredene. He died on 10 Feb 1932 in Bredene. He was a stoker bij de staatsmarine.
ii. Augustus Desiderius Vansieleghem was born on 11 Mar 1866 in Bredene. He died on 24 Nov 1952 in Oostende.
iii. Eduardus Ludovicus Vansieleghem was born on 20 Apr 1868 in Bredene. He died on 23 Mar 1947 in Oostende. He was ongehuwd.
iv. Petrus Jacobus Vansieleghem was born on 21 Oct 1871 in Bredene. He died on 19 Jun 1947 in Leffinge.
v. Sophia Vansieleghem was born on 10 Feb 1876 in Bredene. She died on 29 May 1969 in Zandvoorde.
8 vi. Camillus Josephus Vansieleghem.
vii. Louis Vansieleghem was born on 12 Feb 1879 in Bredene. He died on 24 Jul 1968 in Oostende. He was kinderloos.
viii. Honorine Maria Vansieleghem was born on 13 Jun 1883 in Bredene. She died on 18 Jun 1975 in Oostende. She was ongehuwd.

SIXTH GENERATION

38. Joannes Vandenbroele was born on 18 Jul 1829 in Bredene. He died on 5 Mar 1872 in Bredene. He was married to Catharina Josepha Lauwagie on 3 Aug 1854 in Oostende.

39. Catharina Josepha Lauwagie. Children were:

19 i. Mathilde Vandenbroele.
ii. Carolus Vandenbroele was born on 19 Oct 1855 in Oostende. He died on 30 Jun 1857 in Oostende.
iii. Ludovica Vandenbroele was born in 1856.
iv. Ferdinand Vandenbroele was born on 21 Feb 1859 in Oostende. He died on 1 Dec 1859 in Oostende.
v. Ferdinand Vandenbroele was born in 1860. He was born on 10 Apr 1860 in Oostende.
vi. Clementia Vandenbroele was born in 1862.

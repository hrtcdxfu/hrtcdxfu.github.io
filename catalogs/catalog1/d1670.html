NINTH GENERATION

304. Josephus Vandenbroele was born in 1712 in Bredene. He died in 1768. He was married to Maria Godelieve Deprez in 1753 in Gistel.

305. Maria Godelieve Deprez died on 23 Feb 1773 in Bredene. Children were:

i. Jan Baptist Vandenbroele was born on 21 Sep 1754 in Bredene. He died on 31 Jul 1755 in Bredene.
ii. Francisca Vandenbroele was born on 9 May 1756 in Bredene.
iii. Petronella Vandenbroele was born on 27 Jun 1758 in Bredene. She died on 10 Nov 1765 in Bredene.
iv. Helena Victoria Vandenbroele was born on 2 Oct 1759 in Bredene. She died on 2 Nov 1760 in Bredene.
v. Ludovicus Vandenbroele was born on 2 Nov 1760 in Bredene. He died on 4 Sep 1761 in Bredene.
vi. Joannes Vandenbroele was born on 23 Mar 1763 in Bredene.
152 vii. Richard Vandenbroele.
viii. Leopoldus Vandenbroele was born on 14 Nov 1768 in Bredene. He died on 6 Oct 1819 in Klemskerke.

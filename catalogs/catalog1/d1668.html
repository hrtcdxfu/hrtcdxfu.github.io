EIGHTH GENERATION

152. Richard Vandenbroele was born on 25 Oct 1765 in Bredene. He died on 28 Jun 1856 in Klemskerke. he had 15 chidren; 3 died very young; 12 survived, 6 boys & 6 girls He was married to Agnes Dedecker on 9 Oct 1790 in Klemskerke.

SIXTH GENERATION

44. Pertrus Jacobus Vanhee was baptised on 16 Dec 1795 in Koekelare. He was born on 16 Dec 1795 in Koekelare. He died on 11 Apr 1859 in Eernegem. He was married to Sophia Beuselinck on 19 Jan 1826 in Eernegem.

45. Sophia Beuselinck was born on 8 Apr 1801 in Eernegem. She died on 28 May 1870 in Eernegem. Children were:

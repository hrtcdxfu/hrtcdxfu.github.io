EIGHTH GENERATION

128. Judocus Vansieleghem was born on 10 Apr 1726 in Ichtegem. He died after 1772 in Ichtegem. He was a Baljuw van Ichtegem en Koekelare ambacht. He was married to Maria Anna Parmentier on 30 Apr 1754 in Ichtegem.

129. Maria Anna Parmentier was born on 26 Feb 1730 in Ichtegem. She died on 26 Mar 1801 in Kortemark. Children were:

64 i. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem.
ii. Isabella Clara Vansieleghem was born on 15 Aug 1756 in Ichtegem.
iii. Petrus Judocus Vansieleghem was born on 26 Aug 1758 in Ichtegem.
iv. Carolus Benedictus Vansieleghem was born on 12 Mar 1761 in Ichtegem.
v. Maria Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 21 Aug 1763 in Ichtegem.
vi. Isabella Georgia Vansieleghem was born on 27 Jan 1766 in Ichtegem.
vii. Maria Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 2 Apr 1768 in Ichtegem. She died on 20 Mar 1813.
viii. Joanna Clara Colleta Vansieleghem was born on 16 Aug 1769 in Ichtegem.
ix. Francisca Colleta Vansieleghem was born on 7 Mar 1772 in Ichtegem.

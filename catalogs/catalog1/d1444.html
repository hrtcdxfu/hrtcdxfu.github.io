EIGHTH GENERATION

176. Ludovicus Joannes Vanhee was born on 18 May 1732 in Eernegem. He was baptised on 18 May 1732 in Eernegem. He died on 6 May 1804 in Eernegem. He was married to Maria Catharina Strubbe on 13 May 1758 in Snellegem.

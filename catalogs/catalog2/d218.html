SEVENTH GENERATION

375. Johanna Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 15 Apr 1795 in Leffinge. She died on 22 Nov 1879 in Leffinge. She was a notaris.

She was married to Carolus Verhulst (son of Michel Francois Eugene Verhulst and Marie-Therese van der Haeghen) on 19 Jan 1824 in Leffinge. Carolus Verhulst was born on 5 Mar 1794 in Lichtervelde. He was a notaris between 1828 and 1860 in Leffinge. He died on 21 Apr 1862 in Leffinge. Johanna Theresia Vansieleghem and Carolus Verhulst had the following children:

531 i. Charles Francois Verhulst was born on 27 Dec 1824 in Leffinge. He died on 13 Apr 1854 in Brugge.
532 ii. Justina Carolina Verhulst was born on 24 Apr 1827 in Leffinge. She died on 28 Apr 1830 in Leffinge.

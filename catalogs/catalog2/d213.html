SEVENTH GENERATION

370. Joseph Carolus Franciscus Vansieleghem was born on 14 Oct 1788 in Leffinge. He was a notaris Koekelare between 1816 and 1833 in Koekelare. He was a notaris Brugge between 1833 and 1858 in Brugge. He died on 1 Apr 1861 in Eernegem.

He was married to Marie Theresia Questier de ter Dhont (daughter of Joannes Franciscus Questier and Maria Theresia Vansieleghem) on 5 Aug 1812 in Eernegem. Marie Theresia Questier de ter Dhont was born on 13 May 1793 in Eernegem. She died on 21 Jun 1847 in Eernegem.

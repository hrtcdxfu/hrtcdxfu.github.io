NINTH GENERATION

587. Omer Vanzieleghem was born on 19 Jun 1911 in Eernegem. He died on 7 Mar 1982 in Eernegem. He was a diamantslijper.

He was married to Magdalena Sidonia Decorte in 1935 in Eernegem. Magdalena Sidonia Decorte was born on 8 Mar 1914 in Eernegem. She died on 7 Mar 1982 in Eernegem. She died on 29 Aug 1988 in Eernegem. Omer Vanzieleghem and Magdalena Sidonia Decorte had the following children:

+749 i. Lisette Vanzieleghem.
+750 ii. Diana Vanzieleghem.
+751 iii. Michel Vanzieleghem.
752 iv. Robert Vanzieleghem (Private).

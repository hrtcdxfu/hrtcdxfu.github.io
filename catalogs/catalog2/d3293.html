FIFTH GENERATION

139. Maria Anna Van Zieleghem was born on 16 Sep 1728 in Zwevezele, Brugge District, W. Flanders, Belgium. She died on 23 Jun 1797 in Koolskamp. She has Ancestral File number 13L9-MF9.

She was married to Florentius Jacobus Demeulenaere on 3 May 1748 in Koolskamp. Florentius Jacobus Demeulenaere was born on 25 Oct 1718 in Koolskamp. He died on 14 Feb 1803 in Koolskamp. Maria Anna Van Zieleghem and Florentius Jacobus Demeulenaere had the following children:

189 i. Gregorius Augustinus Demeulenaere was born on 1 Apr 1749 in Koolskamp. He died on 10 Jan 1758 in Koolskamp.
190 ii. Ludovicus Josephus Demeulenaere was born on 12 Feb 1750/51 in Koolskamp. He died on 22 Jul 1759 in Koolskamp.
191 iii. Emanuel Jacobus Demeulenaere was born on 6 Apr 1753 in Koolskamp. He died in Passendale.
192 iv. Ferdinandus Demeulenaere was born on 30 May 1755 in Koolskamp. He died on 28 Dec 1835 in Koolskamp.
193 v. Maria Anna Demeulenaere was born on 7 May 1757 in Koolskamp. She died on 5 Apr 1758 in Koolskamp.
194 vi. Dominicus Demeulenaere was born on 30 Oct 1759 in Koolskamp. He died on 24 Jan 1827 in Koolskamp.
195 vii. Franciscus Demeulenaere was born in 1762 in Koolskamp. He died on 29 Aug 1770 in Koolskamp.
196 viii. Petrus Augustinus Demeulenaere was born on 31 May 1764 in Koolskamp.
197 ix. Veronica Josepha Demeulenaere was born on 6 Jan 1767 in Koolskamp.
198 x. Francisca Demeulenaere was born on 30 May 1769 in Koolskamp. She died on 30 Aug 1770 in Koolskamp.
199 xi. Franciscus Demeulenaere was born on 3 Jun 1771 in Koolskamp.
200 xii. Ivo Demeulenaere was born on 6 Apr 1774 in Koolskamp. He died on 1 Jan 1778 in Koolskamp.

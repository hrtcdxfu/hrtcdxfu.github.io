THIRD GENERATION

26. Michiel Adriaan Vansieleghem was born on 6 Apr 1684 in Lichtervelde. He died on 4 Jan 1742 in Torhout. He was st.v.g. R.A.Brugge Lichtervelde n° 1545 on 21 Mar 1744 in Lichtervelde. He was Wezenakte reg 355 F 227 akte van on 21 Mar 1744 in Lichtervelde. He was hier begint stam F Frans Vansieleghem Lichtervelde.

He was married to Jossijne Vereenooghe on 8 Feb 1710 in Lichtervelde. Michiel Adriaan Vansieleghem and Jossijne Vereenooghe had the following children:

+71 i. Michael Vansieleghem.
72 ii. Anna Maria Vansieleghem was born on 16 Apr 1714 in Zwevezele.
+73 iii. Joannes Baptistus Vansieleghem.
+74 iv. Veronica Vansieleghem.
+75 v. Joanna Barbara Vansieleghem.
76 vi. Petronella Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 1 Dec 1727 in Torhout.

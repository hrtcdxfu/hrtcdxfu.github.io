EIGHTH GENERATION

+593 i. Felicie Vansieleghem.
594 ii. Benoit Vansieleghem was born on 12 Oct 1904 in Stene. He died on 12 Dec 1904 in Stene.
595 iii. Elza Maria Vansieleghem (Private).
+596 iv. Theophile Van sieleghem.
597 v. Rachel Van Sieleghem was born on 8 Mar 1908 in Zerkegem. She died 3/5/ in Koekelare.
+598 vi. Laura Vansieleghem.

NINTH GENERATION

625. Margueritte Vansieleghem was born on 11 Jan 1903 in Handzame. She died on 15 Feb 1988 in Oostende.

She was married to Alidoor Ampe (son of Carolus Ludovicus Ampe and Mathilde Justina Selschotter) on 6 Jan 1925 in Oostende. Alidoor Ampe was born on 2 Feb 1896 in Handzame. He died on 29 Nov 1966 in Oostende. He was a slager.

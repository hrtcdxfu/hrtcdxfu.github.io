618. Maria Vansieleghem (Private).

Richard Vervisch (son of Alfons Vervisch and Idalie Timmerman) was born on 8 Jun 1915 in Sint Kruis. He died on 20 Oct 1985 in Brugge. He was a farmer. Maria Vansieleghem and Richard Vervisch had the following children:

+814 i. Gerard Vervisch.

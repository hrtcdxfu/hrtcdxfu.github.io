FOURTH GENERATION

71. Michael Vansieleghem was born on 18 Dec 1710 in Zwevezele. He died on 13 Sep 1756 in Torhout.

He was married to Anna Theresia Tommelin on 6 May 1738 in Lichtervelde. Anna Theresia Tommelin was born in 1714 in Lichtervelde. She died on 25 Dec 1787 in Torhout. Michael Vansieleghem and Anna Theresia Tommelin had the following children:

SECOND GENERATION

7. Josephus Van Sieleghem was born on 26 Jan 1638 in Lichtervelde. He died on 14 May 1692 in Lichtervelde. He was RA Brugge archief Lichtervelde reg; nr.353 on 15 Nov 1710 in Lichtervelde.

He was married to Maria Lansens (daughter of Paulus Lampsens and Clara Lanssens) on 12 Aug 1657 in Lichtervelde. Maria Lansens was born on 4 Mar 1637 in Lichtervelde. She died on 2 Sep 1709 in Lichtervelde. Josephus Van Sieleghem and Maria Lansens had the following children:

EIGHTH GENERATION

533. Hippolyte Louis Jean Verhulst was born on 18 Oct 1832 in Leffinge. He died on 27 Feb 1878 in Evere.

He was married to Hortense Clemence Maerle. Hortense Clemence Maerle died on 20 Feb 1867 in Poperinge.

He was married to Elisa Dreser (daughter of Francois Dreser and Rosalie van Riebeke) on 10 Jan 1870 in Leffinge. Elisa Dreser was born on 28 Mar 1849 in Snaaskerke. She died on 31 Jul 1877.

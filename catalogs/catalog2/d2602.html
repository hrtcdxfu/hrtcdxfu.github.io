EIGHTH GENERATION

479. Henri Benoit Vanzieleghem was born on 3 Jan 1875 in Lichtervelde. He died on 10 Sep 1945 in Brussel. He was a carpenter. moved to
Brussel 20/09/1897

He was married to Marguerite Charlier (daughter of Jan Charlier and Maria Elisabeth Cramer) on 2 Apr 1902 in Sint Joost ten Node. Marguerite Charlier was born on 7 Jan 1874 in Poekem. She died on 20 Feb 1964 in Brussel. Henri Benoit Vanzieleghem and Marguerite Charlier had the following children:

+642 i. Leonard Henri Vanzieleghem.
643 ii. Sophie Maria Vanzieleghem was born on 9 Jan 1903 in St. Joost ten Node. She died on 18 Feb 1929 in Brussel.
644 iii. Marie Marguerite Vanzieleghem was born on 16 Nov 1914 in Jette. She was christened on 21 Nov 1914. She died on 1 Sep 1915 in Jette.

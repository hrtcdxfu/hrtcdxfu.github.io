SEVENTH GENERATION

351. Rosalia Vansieleghem was born on 16 Aug 1822 in Lichtervelde. She died on 22 Jun 1899 in Lichtervelde. She was unmarried. She was a knitter. ongehuwd

Rosalia Vansieleghem had the following children:

458 i. Carolus Ludovicus Vanzieleghem was born on 21 Sep 1847 in Zwevezele. He died on 25 Nov 1847 in Lichtervelde.
459 ii. Julie Vanzieleghem was born on 23 Mar 1849 in Zwevezele. She died on 23 Feb 1931 in Lichtervelde. She was a kloosterzuster.

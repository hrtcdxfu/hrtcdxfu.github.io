THIRD GENERATION

42. Pasclesius Vansieleghem was born on 28 Feb 1664 in Lichtervelde. He died on 4 Jan 1695 in Lichtervelde. He was St.v.g. Ra Brugge Arch. Lichtervelde 352 in Lichtervelde.

He was married to Jacoba Minne on 12 Feb 1686 in Lichtervelde. Pasclesius Vansieleghem and Jacoba Minne had the following children:

82 i. Petrus Vansieleghem was born on 22 Jan 1687 in Lichtervelde. He was born on 22 Jan 1687 in Lichtervelde. He died on 27 Nov 1689 in Lichtervelde.
83 ii. Laurentius Vansieleghem was born on 11 Apr 1688 in Lichtervelde.
84 iii. Maria Joanna Vansieleghem was born on 3 Apr 1690 in Lichtervelde.
85 iv. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 24 Apr 1692 in Lichtervelde.
86 v. Joseph Vansieleghem was born on 4 Jan 1694 in Lichtervelde.

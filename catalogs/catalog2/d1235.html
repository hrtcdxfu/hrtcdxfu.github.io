SEVENTH GENERATION

317. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 13 Apr 1829 in Torhout. He died on 14 Feb 1890 in Torhout. He was a werkman, koopman in regenschermen herbergier - slachter.

He was married to Rosalia Lagacie in 1856. Rosalia Lagacie died on 20 Apr 1883 in Torhout. She was born in Ichtegem. Joannes Vansieleghem and Rosalia Lagacie had the following children:

408 i. Eduard Vansieleghem was born on 18 Dec 1857 in Torhout. He died in 1858.
409 ii. Eduard Vansieleghem was born on 2 Feb 1859 in Torhout.
410 iii. Silvie Vansieleghem was born on 2 Feb 1859 in Torhout.
+411 iv. Petrus Franciscus Vansieleghem.
+412 v. Francis Theophile Vansieleghem.
413 vi. Aloysius Vansieleghem was born on 18 Jun 1866 in Torhout.
+414 vii. Eugenie Vansieleghem.

Marie Louise Ghijsels was born in Ichtegem. She died in Torhout. Joannes Vansieleghem and Marie Louise Ghijsels had the following children:

+415 i. Irma Marie Jeanne Vansieleghem.

NINTH GENERATION

610. Sylvia Vansieleghem was born on 22 Jul 1895 in Aartrijke. She died on 11 Apr 1968 in Woumen.

She was married to Leon Devisschere in 1925 in Torhout. Leon Devisschere was born on 15 May 1895 in Ardooie. He died on 11 Oct 1978 in Woumen. He was a farmer.

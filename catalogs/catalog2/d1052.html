NINTH GENERATION

606. Eugenia Vansieleghem was born on 13 May 1889 in Aartrijke. She died on 23 Nov 1961 in Bovekerke.

She was married to Charles Callewaert in 1920 in Torhout. Charles Callewaert was born on 12 May 1888 in Lichtervelde. He died on 27 Dec 1968 in Gits. Eugenia Vansieleghem and Charles Callewaert had the following children:

+771 i. Noëlla Callewaert.

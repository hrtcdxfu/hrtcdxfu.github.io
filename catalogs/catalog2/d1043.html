NINTH GENERATION

627. Martha Maria Antonia Vansieleghem was born on 28 Oct 1912 in Oostende. She died on 30 Jul 2000 in Oostende.

She was married to Maurice Gustaaf Werbrouck on 7 Jun 1932 in Oostende. Maurice Gustaaf Werbrouck was born on 21 Jan 1908 in Oostende. He died in Mar 1997 in Oostende.

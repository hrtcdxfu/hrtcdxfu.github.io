SIXTH GENERATION

250. Petrus Vanzieleghem was born on 8 Oct 1795 in Lichtervelde. He died on 2 Oct 1878 in Lichtervelde. He was a landlord-shopkeeper.

He was married to Barbara De Peckere on 19 Jan 1825 in Lichtervelde. Barbara De Peckere was born on 30 Apr 1798 in Zwevezele. She died on 4 Feb 1855 in Lichtervelde. Petrus Vanzieleghem and Barbara De Peckere had the following children:

+356 i. Rosalia Vanzieleghem.
+357 ii. Eduardus Vanzieleghem.
358 iii. Elisabeth Vanzieleghem was born on 5 Dec 1830 in Lichtervelde. She died on 19 Jun 1831 in Lichtervelde.
+359 iv. Lucia Vanzieleghem.
+360 v. Petrus Vanzieleghem.
361 vi. Marie Louise Vanzieleghem was born on 14 Apr 1837 in Lichtervelde.
362 vii. Felix Vanzieleghem was born on 1 Jul 1838 in Lichtervelde.
363 viii. Philomena Vanzieleghem was born on 11 Jul 1839 in Lichtervelde. She died on 26 May 1840 in Lichtervelde.
364 ix. Henricus Augustus Vanzieleghem was born on 28 Sep 1841 in Lichtervelde. He died on 16 Oct 1841 in Lichtervelde.
365 x. Philomena Vanzieleghem was born on 20 Mar 1844 in Lichtervelde. She died on 5 Apr 1845 in Lichtervelde.

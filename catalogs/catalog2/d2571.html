SEVENTH GENERATION

353. Melania Vanzieleghem was born on 4 Feb 1829 in Lichtervelde. She died on 14 Oct 1918 in Lichtervelde. She was a herbergierster.

She was married to Frederic Dely on 10 Jun 1863 in Lichtervelde. Frederic Dely was born on 7 Dec 1830 in Torhout. He died on 11 Feb 1914. He was a dagloner. Melania Vanzieleghem and Frederic Dely had the following children:

470 i. Maria Sidonie Vanzieleghem was born on 18 Feb 1859 in Lichtervelde. She died on 12 Jun 1859 in Lichtervelde.

THIRD GENERATION

56. Eleonora Joanna Vansieleghem was born on 9 Nov 1680 in Lichtervelde. She died on 27 May 1742 in Lissewege.

Cesarius Slabbynck died in Bredene.

She was married to Petrus De Brock on 12 May 1716 in Bredene. Petrus De Brock was born on 22 Dec 1682 in Oudenburg. He died on 9 Oct 1748 in Lissewege. Eleonora Joanna Vansieleghem and Petrus De Brock had the following children:

134 i. Jacobus De Brock was born on 27 Feb 1717 in Bredene.
135 ii. Michael De Brock was born on 21 Jun 1726 in Lissewege. He died on 12 Jan 1776 in Lissewege.

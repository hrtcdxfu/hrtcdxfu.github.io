FIFTH GENERATION

165. Genoveva Inghelbrecht was born on 22 Mar 1738 in Ichtegem. She died on 8 Jun 1804 in Eernegem.

She was married to Leonardus Inghelbrecht on 10 Jul 1759 in Eernegem. Leonardus Inghelbrecht was born on 12 May 1722 in Eernegem. He died on 7 May 1803 in Eernegem. Genoveva Inghelbrecht and Leonardus Inghelbrecht had the following children:

+252 i. Petrus Inghelbrecht.
253 ii. Maria Godelieva Inghelbrecht was born on 17 Sep 1760 in Eernegem. She died on 18 Oct 1799 in Eernegem.

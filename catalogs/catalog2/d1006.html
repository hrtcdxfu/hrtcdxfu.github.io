NINTH GENERATION

688. Joseph François Auguste Vansieleghem was born on 19 Mar 1867 in Eernegem. He died on 17 Sep 1947 in Eernegem. He was a brouwer.

He was married to Blondine Melanie Justine Michens (daughter of Victor Michens and Melanie Justine Verhoest) on 7 Oct 1890 in Oostende. Blondine Melanie Justine Michens was born on 28 May 1865 in Oostende. She died on 16 Jan 1925 in Eernegem.

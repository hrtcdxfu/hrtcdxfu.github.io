THIRD GENERATION

53. Joosijne Vansieleghem was born on 9 Feb 1672 in Lichtervelde. She died on 1 Jul 1739 in Zedelgem.

She was married to Petrus Van Hijlenbrouck on 18 Oct 1704 in Lichtervelde. Joosijne Vansieleghem and Petrus Van Hijlenbrouck had the following children:

116 i. Maria Godelieve Van Hijlenbrouck was born on 16 Oct 1689 in Lichtervelde.
117 ii. Jacobus Van Hijlenbrouck was born on 25 Jun 1691 in Lichtervelde.
118 iii. Egidius Van Hijlenbrouck was born on 10 Oct 1694 in Lichtervelde.
119 iv. Petronella Clara Van Hijlenbrouck was born on 13 Apr 1697 in Lichtervelde.

SEVENTH GENERATION

268. Maria Anna Van Sieleghem was born on 23 May 1775 in Zwevezele. She died on 9 Feb 1844 in Ruddervoorde. She was volkstelling 1814 (V.V.F II 2b 499) ballie Brugge Ruddervoorde. She has Ancestral File number 13L9-9FK.

She was married to Karel Slabbinck (son of Jan-Baptiste Slabbinck and Regina Neels) on 17 Feb 1808 in Zwevezele. She was married to Karel Slabbinck. Karel Slabbinck was born on 17 Feb 1766 in Ruddervoorde. He died on 3 Oct 1823 in Ruddervoorde. Maria Anna Van Sieleghem and Karel Slabbinck had the following children:

+378 i. Franciscus Slabbinck.
379 ii. Joannes Slabbinck was born on 7 Nov 1810 in Ruddervoorde. He died on 4 Sep 1823 in Ruddervoorde.
+380 iii. Juliana Slabbinck.
381 iv. Jozef Slabbinck was born on 18 Jul 1815 in Ruddervoorde. He died on 25 Nov 1816 in Ruddervoorde.
+382 v. Pieter Slabbinck.

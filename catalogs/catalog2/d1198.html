NINTH GENERATION

577. René Alfons Vansieleghem was born on 2 Feb 1893 in Eernegem. He died on 20 Oct 1978 in Oostende. He was a kleermaker.

He was married to Pharailde Vanmassenhove (daughter of Charles Vanmassenhove and Rosalia Verhelle) in 1918 in Eernegem. Pharailde Vanmassenhove was born on 25 Oct 1897 in Eernegem. She died on 31 Oct 1983 in Oostende. René Alfons Vansieleghem and Pharailde Vanmassenhove had the following children:

+737 i. Michel Vansieleghem.
+738 ii. Rachel Angele Vansieleghem.
+739 iii. Emiel Karel Vansieleghem.

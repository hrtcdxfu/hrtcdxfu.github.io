682. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem was born on 4 Aug 1841 in Slijpe. He was born on 14 Aug 1841 in Slijpe. He was a mayor between 1888 and 1902 in Slijpe. He died on 5 Sep 1918 in Brugge. He was a farmer.

He was married to Mathilda Vermeire (daughter of Joannes Vermeire and Theresia Kesteloot) on 9 Nov 1870 in Stene. Mathilda Vermeire was born on 14 Sep 1849 in Stene. She died on 26 Sep 1892 in Slijpe. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem and Mathilda Vermeire had the following children:

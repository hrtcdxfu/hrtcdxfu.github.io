TENTH GENERATION

778. Frans Joseph Vansieleghem was born on 9 Jul 1921 in Wingene. He died on 17 Feb 1992 in Oostende.

He was married to Gabrielle Reynaert in 1953 in Bovekerke. Frans Joseph Vansieleghem and Gabrielle Reynaert had the following children:

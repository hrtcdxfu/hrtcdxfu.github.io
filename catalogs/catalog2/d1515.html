SEVENTH GENERATION

346. Marie-Louise Vansieleghem was born on 12 Apr 1839 in Torhout. She died on 25 Nov 1889 in Ichtegem.

She was married to Ludovicus Franciscus Verhelst (son of Cornelius Verhelst and Rosalie Montaigne) on 7 Jul 1886 in Torhout. Ludovicus Franciscus Verhelst was born on 1 Jul 1840 in Lichtervelde.

NINTH GENERATION

707. Carolus Franciscus Vansieleghem was born on 22 Jan 1863 in Bredene. He died on 10 Feb 1932 in Bredene. He was a stoker bij de staatsmarine.

He was married to Maria Virginia Vanhoucke. Maria Virginia Vanhoucke was born on 3 Feb 1871 in Oostende. She died in Brussel. Carolus Franciscus Vansieleghem and Maria Virginia Vanhoucke had the following children:

915 i. Louise Vansieleghem was born on 31 Aug 1890 in Bredene. She died on 5 Dec 1968 in Oostende. She was ongehuwd.
916 ii. Carolus Franciscus Vansieleghem was born in 1893 in Bredene. He died on 13 Jun 1893 in Stene. He was niet gedoopt.
917 iii. Carolus Ludovicus Vansieleghem was born on 29 Mar 1896 in Bredene. He died on 23 Sep 1896 in Oostende.
918 iv. Irma Angèle Vansieleghem was born on 2 Aug 1897 in Oostende. She died on 26 May 1898 in Oostende.
+919 v. Charlotte Vansieleghem.

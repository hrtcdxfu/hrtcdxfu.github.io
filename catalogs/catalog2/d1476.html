SIXTH GENERATION

242. Leopold Franciscus Vansieleghem was born on 25 Oct 1795 in Torhout. He died on 30 Jan 1867 in Torhout. He was a wever.

He was married to Marie Jeanne Vande Bussche in Aartrijke. Marie Jeanne Vande Bussche was born in Aartrijke. Leopold Franciscus Vansieleghem and Marie Jeanne Vande Bussche had the following children:

+336 i. Lodewijk Vansieleghem.
+337 ii. Severinus Vansieleghem.
+338 iii. Constantijn Vansieleghem.
+339 iv. Rosalia Vansieleghem.

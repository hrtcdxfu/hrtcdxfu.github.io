NINTH GENERATION

683. Philomena Sophia Vansieleghem was born on 1 Jan 1845 in Slijpe. She died on 17 Sep 1877 in Leffinge.

She was married to Carolus Ludovicus Pruvost on 20 Jan 1864 in Slijpe. Carolus Ludovicus Pruvost was born on 31 Mar 1828 in Leffinge. He died on 23 Oct 1902 in Leffinge. He was a farmer. Philomena Sophia Vansieleghem and Carolus Ludovicus Pruvost had the following children:

+878 i. Emiel Carel Jozef Provoost.
+879 ii. Elisa Justina Provoost.
+880 iii. Leon Augustus Pruvost.
+881 iv. Josephus Franciscus Pruvost.

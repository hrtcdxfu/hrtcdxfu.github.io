NINTH GENERATION

713. Louis Vansieleghem was born on 12 Feb 1879 in Bredene. He died on 24 Jul 1968 in Oostende. He was kinderloos.

He was married to Clementia Prudentia Burke (daughter of Desiderius Ludovicus Burke and Delphina Christina Joanna Lippens) on 24 May 1899 in Bredene. Clementia Prudentia Burke was born on 9 Oct 1879 in Bredene. She died on 25 Apr 1957 in Oostende.

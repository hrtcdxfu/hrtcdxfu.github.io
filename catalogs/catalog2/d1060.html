NINTH GENERATION

615. Hector Vansieleghem was born on 12 Sep 1904 in Torhout. He died in Oct 1984 in Roeselare. He was a farmer.

Margriet Godderis was born on 14 Mar 1909 in Moorslede. She died on 24 Feb 1962 in Beitem. Hector Vansieleghem and Margriet Godderis had the following children:

+809 i. Monique Vansieleghem.
+810 ii. Rika Vansieleghem.

He was married to Germaine Goethals in 1969 in Roeselare. Germaine Goethals was born on 17 Sep 1910 in Beveren-Roeselare. She died on 2 Nov 1982 in Roeselare.

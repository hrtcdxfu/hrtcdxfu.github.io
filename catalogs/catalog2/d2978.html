FIFTH GENERATION

138. Petrus Joseph Van Zieleghem was born on 29 Aug 1726 in Zwevezele, Brugge District, W. Flanders, Belgium. He died on 29 Dec 1785 in Zwevezele, Brugge District, W. Flanders, Belgium. He has Ancestral File number 13L9-N5S.

He was married to Maria Francisca Van Dierendonck (daughter of Jan Van Dierendonck and Godelieve Van de Wiele) on 2 May 1754 in Zwevezele, Brugge District, W. Flanders, Belgium. Maria Francisca Van Dierendonck was born on 16 Jun 1728 in Zwevezele, Brugge District, W. Flanders, Belgium. She died on 26 Oct 1795 in Zwevezele, Brugge District, W. Flanders, Belgium.

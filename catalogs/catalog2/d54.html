THIRD GENERATION

44. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 18 Jun 1670 in Lichtervelde. He died on 16 Apr 1721 in Aartrijke. He was boedelbeschrijving 1068 on 29 Oct 1721 in Lichtervelde.

He was married to Maria Boussaert on 6 Jul 1700 in Aartrijke. Joannes Vansieleghem and Maria Boussaert had the following children:

87 i. Amelia Vansieleghem was born on 1 May 1700 in Aartrijke.
88 ii. Adriaen Vansieleghem was born on 14 Mar 1704 in Aartrijke.
+89 iii. Pieter Vansieleghem.
90 iv. Anna Marie Vansieleghem was born in 1709 in Aartrijke.
91 v. Joanna Vansieleghem was born in 1701 in Aartrijke. She was born in 1716 in Aartrijke. She died young.

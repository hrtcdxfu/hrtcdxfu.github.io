TENTH GENERATION

934. Valère Karel Balliere was born on 26 May 1917 in Oostende. He died on 17 Jul 2000 in Oudenburg. He was a meester-kleermaker.

He was married to Angèle Irène Bogaert on 28 Aug 1945 in Zandvoorde. Angèle Irène Bogaert was born on 3 Aug 1919 in Zandvoorde. She died on 14 Nov 1992 in Oostende. Valère Karel Balliere and Angèle Irène Bogaert had the following children:

+1170 i. Jan Karel Balliere.

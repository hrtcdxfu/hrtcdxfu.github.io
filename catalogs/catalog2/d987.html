NINTH GENERATION

588. Maria Emma Vansieleghem was born on 6 Aug 1908 in Eernegem. She died on 16 Jun 1977 in Eernegem.

She was married to Etienne Nyssen (son of Arthur Nyssen and Maria Monteyne) in 1933 in Eernegem. Etienne Nyssen was born on 22 Feb 1910 in Eernegem. He died on 9 Mar 1986 in Oostende. He was a schilder - behanger - winkelier.

TENTH GENERATION

737. Michel Vansieleghem was born on 30 Aug 1918 in Eernegem. He died on 8 Dec 1964 in Brugge. He was a belastingsverificateur.

He was married to Georgette Maria Vandenberghe (daughter of Carolus Ludovicus Vandenberghe and Devogelaere) on 19 Jun 1945 in Bekegem. Michel Vansieleghem and Georgette Maria Vandenberghe had the following children:

+960 i. Paul Martin René Marie Vansieleghem.
+961 ii. Anneliese Dominica Caroline Maria Vansieleghem.

NINTH GENERATION

699. Albertina Eugenia Vansieleghem was born on 1 Nov 1851 in Wilskerke. She died on 19 Mar 1936 in Bredene.

She was married to Emilius Augustus Casteleyn (son of Henricus Josephus Casteleyn and Rosalia Francisca Vanryssel) on 10 Apr 1875 in Oostende. Emilius Augustus Casteleyn was born on 4 Apr 1850 in Gistel. Albertina Eugenia Vansieleghem and Emilius Augustus Casteleyn had the following children:

886 i. Lisa Casteleyn.
887 ii. Jeani Casteleyn.

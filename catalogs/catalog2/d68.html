THIRD GENERATION

52. Guillielmus Vansieleghem was born on 30 Dec 1668 in Lichtervelde.

He was married to Anna Hillewaere (daughter of Anthoine Hillewaere) on 22 Sep 1691 in Lichtervelde. Guillielmus Vansieleghem and Anna Hillewaere had the following children:

110 i. Maria Godelieve Vansieleghem was born on 6 Jul 1692 in Lichtervelde.
111 ii. Petronella Vansieleghem was born on 16 Jan 1694 in Lichtervelde.
112 iii. Anna Maria Vansieleghem was born on 12 Oct 1695 in Lichtervelde.
113 iv. Joanna Vansieleghem was born on 6 Jan 1698 in Lichtervelde.
114 v. Petrus Jacobus Vansieleghem was born on 5 May 1703 in Lichtervelde.

TENTH GENERATION

881. Josephus Franciscus Pruvost was born on 23 Apr 1873 in Leffinge.

He was married to Charlotte Jeanne Marie Baey (daughter of Jules Baey and Isabella Pintelon) on 5 Apr 1899 in Oostende. Charlotte Jeanne Marie Baey was born on 20 Jun 1876 in Nieuwpoort.

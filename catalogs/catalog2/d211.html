SEVENTH GENERATION

368. Maria Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 22 Mar 1785 in Leffinge. She died on 22 Mar 1840 in Ieper.

She was married to Engelbert Bouckenaere on 28 Mar 1812 in Leffinge. Engelbert Bouckenaere was born on 28 Dec 1771 in Ramskapelle. He died on 17 Sep 1833 in Ieper. He was a merchant.

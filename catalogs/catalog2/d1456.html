148. Josephus Vansieleghem was born on 21 May 1747 in Torhout. He died on 19 Apr 1794 in Torhout. Peter: Carolus Viaene
Meter: Veronica Tommelin

He was married to Anna Maria De Vos on 26 May 1782 in Brugge. Anna Maria De Vos was born in 1750 in Torhout. Josephus Vansieleghem and Anna Maria De Vos had the following children:

216 i. Carolus Fr. Vansieleghem was born on 8 Aug 1782 in Torhout.
217 ii. Amelia Carolina Vansieleghem was born on 3 Jun 1784 in Torhout. She died in 1786 in Torhout.
218 iii. Maria Joanna Vansieleghem was born on 9 Feb 1786 in Torhout.
+219 iv. Amelia Vansieleghem.

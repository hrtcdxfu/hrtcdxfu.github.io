FOURTH GENERATION

115. Jacoba Vansieleghem was born on 24 Feb 1736 in Snellegem. She died on 24 Feb 1767 in Snellegem.

She was married to Eduardus Van Rafelghem on 4 May 1762 in Zedelgem. She was married to Eduardus Van Rafelghem. Eduardus Van Rafelghem was born on 29 Jan 1738 in Snellegem. He died on 10 Nov 1808 in Roksem. Jacoba Vansieleghem and Eduardus Van Rafelghem had the following children:

+162 i. Anna Theresia Van Rafelghem.
163 ii. Victoria Francisca Van Rafelghem was born on 15 Sep 1764 in Snellegem.
164 iii. Genoviva Van Rafelghem was born on 16 Apr 1766 in Snellegem. She was born on 25 Dec 1766 in Snellegem.

NINTH GENERATION

710. Petrus Jacobus Vansieleghem was born on 21 Oct 1871 in Bredene. He died on 19 Jun 1947 in Leffinge.

He was married to Theodorina Josphina Devriendt (daughter of Ludovicus Devriendt and Virginia Barbara Luca) on 6 Aug 1902 in Bredene. Theodorina Josphina Devriendt was born on 24 Nov 1868 in Bredene. She died on 19 Jun 1927 in Leffinge. Petrus Jacobus Vansieleghem and Theodorina Josphina Devriendt had the following children:

+927 i. Marcella Bertha Augusta Vansieleghem.
928 ii. Angela Maria Anna Vansieleghem was born on 5 Sep 1904 in Leffinge. She died on 6 May 1941 in Brugge. She was an onderwijzeres. She was ongehuwd.

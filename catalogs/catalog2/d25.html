SECOND GENERATION

3. Michiel Van Sieleghem was born in 1640 in Lichtervelde. He died on 24 May 1679 in Lichtervelde. He died on 24 Mar 1699 in Lichtervelde.

He was married to Joanna Verscheure on 1 Jun 1673 in Lichtervelde. Michiel Van Sieleghem and Joanna Verscheure had the following children:

27 i. Michael Vansieleghem was born on 22 Nov 1677 in Lichtervelde.
28 ii. Georgius Vansieleghem was born on 3 Mar 1679 in Lichtervelde.
29 iii. Carolus Vansieleghem was born on 28 Sep 1680 in Lichtervelde.
30 iv. Joannes B. Vansieleghem was born on 25 Jan 1685 in Lichtervelde.
31 v. Guillielmus Vansieleghem was born on 10 Dec 1686 in Lichtervelde.
32 vi. Martinus Vansieleghem was born on 6 Mar 1689 in Lichtervelde.
33 vii. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 17 Dec 1691 in Lichtervelde. He was geen nageslacht bekend.

He was married to Joanna Lampsens (daughter of Paulus Lampsens and Clara Lanssens) on 8 Oct 1662 in Lichtervelde. Joanna Lampsens was born on 2 Feb 1643 in Lichtervelde. She died on 25 Jan 1670 in Lichtervelde. Michiel Van Sieleghem and Joanna Lampsens had the following children:

THIRD GENERATION

49. Adrianus Vansieleghem was born on 4 May 1661 in Lichtervelde. He died on 3 Feb 1712 in Ardooie.

He was married to Jacoba Mustreel on 7 Apr 1682 in Lichtervelde. Adrianus Vansieleghem and Jacoba Mustreel had the following children:

93 i. Joos Vansieleghem was born in 1683 in Lichtervelde.
94 ii. Joannes Vansieleghem.
95 iii. Adriaan Vansieleghem died on 3 Apr 1794 in Ardooie.
96 iv. Anne Mary Vansieleghem.
+97 v. Catharina Vansieleghem.
98 vi. Michael Vansieleghem.
99 vii. Anna Vansieleghem.

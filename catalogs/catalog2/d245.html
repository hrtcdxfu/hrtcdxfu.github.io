EIGHTH GENERATION

522. Ludovicus Josephus Vansieleghem was born on 8 May 1814 in Leffinge. He died on 30 Jan 1896 in Wilskerke.

He was married to Genoveva Germonpre on 26 Apr 1848 in Leffinge. Genoveva Germonpre was born on 1 Mar 1816 in Leffinge. She died on 28 Jun 1896 in Wilskerke. Ludovicus Josephus Vansieleghem and Genoveva Germonpre had the following children:

+698 i. Louise Maria Vansieleghem.
+699 ii. Albertina Eugenia Vansieleghem.
+700 iii. Carolus Josephus Vansieleghem.
+701 iv. Justina Francisca Vansieleghem.

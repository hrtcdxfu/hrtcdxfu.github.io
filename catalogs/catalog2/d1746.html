SEVENTH GENERATION

316. Pieter Vansieleghem was born on 2 Feb 1828 in Lichtervelde. He died on 18 Feb 1904 in Torhout. He was a werkman.

He was married to Marie Louise Lagacie on 8 Jun 1860 in Ichtegem. Marie Louise Lagacie was born on 18 Jan 1828 in Ichtegem. She died on 2 Nov 1897 in Torhout. Pieter Vansieleghem and Marie Louise Lagacie had the following children:

+403 i. Theophile Vansieleghem.
404 ii. Eugenia Vansieleghem was born on 5 Jan 1863 in Torhout. She died on 2 Aug 1881 in Torhout.
+405 iii. August Vansieleghem.
+406 iv. Amelie Vansieleghem.
+407 v. Elodie Marie Vansieleghem.

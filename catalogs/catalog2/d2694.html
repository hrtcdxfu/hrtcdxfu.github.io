SEVENTH GENERATION

360. Petrus Vanzieleghem was born on 23 Aug 1834 in Lichtervelde. He died on 18 Sep 1888 in Oekene.

He was married to Maria Theresia Ackx on 9 May 1876 in Oekene. Maria Theresia Ackx was born on 19 Oct 1850 in Oekene. She died on 27 Dec 1930 in Oekene. Petrus Vanzieleghem and Maria Theresia Ackx had the following children:

+495 i. Alphonsius Vanzieleghem.
+496 ii. Honoré Vanzieleghem.
497 iii. Helena Maria Juliana Vanzieleghem was born on 11 May 1878 in Oekene. She died on 10 May 1879 in Oekene.
+498 iv. Emilie Marie Vanzieleghem.
+499 v. Valeria Pharailda Vanzieleghem.

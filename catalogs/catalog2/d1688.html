EIGHTH GENERATION

415. Irma Marie Jeanne Vansieleghem was born on 9 May 1890 in Torhout. She died on 26 Feb 1974 in Oostende.

She was married to Maurice Logier (son of August Logier and Amelie D'Haene) on 8 Jan 1913 in Koekelare. Maurice Logier was born on 17 Jul 1887 in Koekelare. He died on 2 Dec 1947 in Koekelare. He was burgemeester van Ichtegem. Irma Marie Jeanne Vansieleghem and Maurice Logier had the following children:

572 i. Roger Jean August Logier (Private).
+573 ii. Joris Maurice Alois Logier.
574 iii. Norbert Arthur Eugène Logier (Private).
+575 iv. Walter Joseph Logier.

EIGHTH GENERATION

506. Maria Josepha Vansieleghem was born on 6 Sep 1819 in Eernegem. She died on 20 Oct 1871 in Oostende.

She was married to Carolus Benedictus Joannes Van Halme on 3 Apr 1844 in Oostende. Carolus Benedictus Joannes Van Halme was born on 4 Apr 1817 in Roeselare. He died on 20 Jan 1866 in Oostende. Maria Josepha Vansieleghem and Carolus Benedictus Joannes Van Halme had the following children:

684 i. Pedro Van Halme was born in 1844 in Eernegem. He died on 21 Nov 1898. He was a notaris te Zedelgem in1878 in Zedelgem.

EIGHTH GENERATION

382. Pieter Slabbinck was born on 4 Feb 1818 in Ruddervoorde. He died on 26 Jul 1849 in Ruddervoorde.

He was married to Maria-Theresia De Meyer on 31 Jul 1844 in Loppem. Maria-Theresia De Meyer was born on 14 Jun 1815 in Loppem. She died on 16 Mar 1886 in Ruddervoorde.

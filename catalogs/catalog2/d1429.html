THIRD GENERATION

25. Marinus Vansieleghem was born on 3 Apr 1681 in Lichtervelde. He died on 25 Dec 1713 in Lichtervelde.

He was married to Elisabeth Wason on 26 Feb 1710 in Lichtervelde. Marinus Vansieleghem and Elisabeth Wason had the following children:

68 i. Marius Vansieleghem was born on 9 Dec 1710 in Lichtervelde. Peter: Josephus Cool
69 ii. Veronica Vansieleghem was born on 8 Aug 1712 in Lichtervelde. Peter: Petrus Vansieleghem
Meter: Martina Vermeulen
70 iii. Judoca Cornelia Vansieleghem was born on 18 Dec 1713 in Lichtervelde. Peter: Guiliamus Wason
Meter: Judoca Vansieleghem

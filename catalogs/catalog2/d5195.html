EIGHTH GENERATION

519. Henri Joseph Vansieleghem was born on 31 Aug 1832 in Ichtegem. He died in Parijs.

He was married to Rosalie Constance Lannoy. Rosalie Constance Lannoy died on 24 Nov 1877 in Parijs.

He was married to Marianna Philomène Ferlicat on 10 Dec 1889 in Ichtegem. Marianna Philomène Ferlicat was born on 15 May 1843 in Plouha-France.

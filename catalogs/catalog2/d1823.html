TENTH GENERATION

754. Frida Maria Cornelia Vansieleghem (Private).

Albert Jozef Bleyaert (son of Michaël Bleyaert and Julia Maria Neirynck) was born on 7 Jun 1934 in Knesselare. He died on 24 Feb 1986 in Oostende. Frida Maria Cornelia Vansieleghem and Albert Jozef Bleyaert had the following children:

986 i. Els Bleyaert (Private).
987 ii. Bart Bleyaert (Private).
988 iii. Kaatje Bleyaert (Private).
989 iv. Koen Bleyaert (Private).

THIRD GENERATION

39. Petrus Vansieleghem was born on 3 May 1656 in Lichtervelde. He was kindren.

He was married to Elisabeth Wijngaert on 12 Aug 1678 in Lichtervelde. van Wijngaert Pieter
Pennick Elisabeth Elisabeth Wijngaert died on 26 May 1732 in Lichtervelde. Petrus Vansieleghem and Elisabeth Wijngaert had the following children:

77 i. Petrus Vansieleghem was born on 24 Feb 1680 in Lichtervelde.
78 ii. Joannes B. Vansieleghem was born on 20 Jan 1682 in Lichtervelde.
79 iii. Jacobus Vansieleghem was born on 16 Oct 1684 in Lichtervelde.
80 iv. Josephus Vansieleghem was born on 28 Jun 1690 in Lichtervelde.
81 v. Adrianus Vansieleghem was born on 1 Sep 1692 in Lichtervelde.

FOURTH GENERATION

89. Pieter Vansieleghem was born in 1707 in Aartrijke. He died on 28 Sep 1780 in Jabbeke.

Cornelia Maene died on 29 Jan 1776 in Jabbeke. She was akte registratie 359 - F° 166V - n° 83 on 5 May 1781 in Lichtervelde. She was weduwe Boye A. familie Guillelimus Maene en Anna De Vinck. Pieter Vansieleghem and Cornelia Maene had the following children:

155 i. Joanna Vansieleghem was born in 1754 in Jabbeke.
156 ii. Isabella Clara Vansieleghem was born in 1755 in Jabbeke.
157 iii. Joannes Jacobus Vansieleghem was born in 1759 in Jabbeke. He died in Mar 1782 in Jabbeke. He was ongehuwd. He was an ambachte van staeten.
158 iv. Pieter Jacobus Vansieleghem was born in 1762 in Jabbeke.
159 v. Anna Maria Vansieleghem was born in 1765 in Jabbeke.
160 vi. Bernard Joseph Vansieleghem was born in 1768 in Jabbeke.
161 vii. Philip Jacob Vansieleghem was born in 1771 in Jabbeke.

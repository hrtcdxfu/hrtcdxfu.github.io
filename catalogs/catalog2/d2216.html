SEVENTH GENERATION

269. Cornelius Vansieleghem was born on 4 Jul 1776 in Zwevezele. He died on 18 Sep 1847 in Zwevezele.

He was married to Rosa Plovie (daughter of Pieter Plovie and Theresia Callewaert) on 1 May 1820 in Zwevezele. Rosa Plovie was born on 20 Jul 1791 in Zwevezele. She died after 1820.

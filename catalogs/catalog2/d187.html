FIFTH GENERATION

172. Judocus Vansieleghem was born on 10 Apr 1726 in Ichtegem. He died after 1772 in Ichtegem. He was a Baljuw van Ichtegem en Koekelare ambacht.

He was married to Maria Anna Parmentier (daughter of Caerel Parmentier and Maria Catharina Beernaert) on 30 Apr 1754 in Ichtegem. Maria Anna Parmentier was born on 26 Feb 1730 in Ichtegem. She died on 26 Mar 1801 in Kortemark. Judocus Vansieleghem and Maria Anna Parmentier had the following children:

+254 i. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem.
255 ii. Isabella Clara Vansieleghem was born on 15 Aug 1756 in Ichtegem.
+256 iii. Petrus Judocus Vansieleghem.
257 iv. Carolus Benedictus Vansieleghem was born on 12 Mar 1761 in Ichtegem.
258 v. Maria Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 21 Aug 1763 in Ichtegem.
259 vi. Isabella Georgia Vansieleghem was born on 27 Jan 1766 in Ichtegem.

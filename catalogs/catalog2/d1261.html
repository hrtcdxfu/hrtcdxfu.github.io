EIGHTH GENERATION

438. Augustinus Vansieleghem was born on 10 Nov 1876 in Torhout. He died on 22 Jun 1961 in Oostende. He was a slager.

He was married to Alida Maria Van Walleghem on 25 Aug 1909 in Torhout. Alida Maria Van Walleghem was born on 30 Oct 1879 in Torhout. She died on 21 Feb 1971 in Oostende. Augustinus Vansieleghem and Alida Maria Van Walleghem had the following children:

+628 i. Maria Ludovica Alicia Vansieleghem.
+629 ii. Yvonne Maria Antonia Vansieleghem.
+630 iii. Simonne Maria Margareta Vansieleghem.

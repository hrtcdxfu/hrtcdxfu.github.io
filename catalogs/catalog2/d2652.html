EIGHTH GENERATION

480. Henricus Mauritius Vanzieleghem was born on 16 Jul 1879 in Lichtervelde. He died on 6 Apr 1947 in Izegem. moved to Roeselare 28/02/1902

He was married to Marie Sidonie Van Bunnen on 12 Feb 1902 in Gent. Marie Sidonie Van Bunnen was born on 20 Jun 1875 in Halluin (Nord). She died in Zwevezele.

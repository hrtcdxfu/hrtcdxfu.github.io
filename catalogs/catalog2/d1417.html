THIRD GENERATION

21. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 27 Feb 1670 in Lichtervelde. He died on 9 Aug 1698 in Zwevezele.

He was married to Martina Vermeulen on 8 Apr 1709 in Lichtervelde.

He was married to Maria Vandeweghe in 1693 in Zwevezele. Maria Vandeweghe was born on 7 Sep 1668 in Zwevezele. She died on 1 Oct 1704 in Zwevezele. Joannes Vansieleghem and Maria Vandeweghe had the following children:

+66 i. Judocus Van Zieleghem.
+67 ii. Joannes Baptiste Vansieleghem.

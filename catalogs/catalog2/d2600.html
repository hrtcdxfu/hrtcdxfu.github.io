EIGHTH GENERATION

477. Victor Vanzieleghem was born on 30 Sep 1870 in Lichtervelde. He died on 24 Oct 1951 in Lichtervelde. He was a butcher-landlord.

He was married to Leonie Sylvia Denys on 28 Oct 1898 in Lichtervelde. Leonie Sylvia Denys was born on 2 Jul 1878 in Lichtervelde. She died on 10 May 1949 in Torhout. Victor Vanzieleghem and Leonie Sylvia Denys had the following children:

+635 i. Michiel Hector Vanzieleghem.
+636 ii. Leonardus Leon Vanzieleghem.
637 iii. Arthur Petrus Vanzieleghem was born on 30 Jan 1901 in Lichtervelde. He died on 28 Oct 1901 in Lichtervelde.
+638 iv. Maria Henrica Vanzieleghem.
639 v. Zulma Maria Alida Vanzieleghem was born on 8 Aug 1906 in Lichtervelde. She died on 4 Mar 1907 in Lichtervelde.
+640 vi. Celine Sylvia Vanzieleghem.
+641 vii. Angèle Laura Vanzieleghem.

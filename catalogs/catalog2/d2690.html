SEVENTH GENERATION

356. Rosalia Vanzieleghem was born on 24 Jul 1827 in Lichtervelde. She was a spinster.

She was married to Albert Roosebeke on 16 Dec 1863 in Ieper. Rosalia Vanzieleghem and Albert Roosebeke had the following children:

493 i. Carolus Ludovicus Vanzieleghem was born on 23 Jul 1850 in Lichtervelde. geboren Vanzieleghem als natuurlijke zoon en na huwelijk gewettigd als Carolus Roosebeke
494 ii. Felix Vanzieleghem was born on 6 Jun 1854 in Lichtervelde. He died on 10 Feb 1856 in Gits.

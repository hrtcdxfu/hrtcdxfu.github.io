EIGHTH GENERATION

426. Alphonsius Maria Vansieleghem was born on 13 Aug 1876 in Eernegem. He died on 19 Jan 1956 in Oostende. He was a kleermaker.

He was married to Leontina Maria Broucke on 22 May 1907 in Eernegem. Leontina Maria Broucke was born on 13 Oct 1881 in Eernegem. She died on 27 Nov 1971 in Oudenburg. Alphonsius Maria Vansieleghem and Leontina Maria Broucke had the following children:

+600 i. Marcel Leopold Vanzieleghem.
+601 ii. Josephus Vanzieleghem.
+602 iii. Marie Josée Alphonsine Vanzieleghem.

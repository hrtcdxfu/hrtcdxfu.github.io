NINTH GENERATION

580. Georges Leopold Vanzieleghem was born on 3 Nov 1900 in Eernegem. He died on 20 Oct 1932 in Oostende. He was a timmerman.

He was married to Emilia Maria Becue on 29 Nov 1923 in Eernegem. Emilia Maria Becue was born on 25 Apr 1904 in Snellegem. She died on 11 Oct 1968 in Oostende. Georges Leopold Vanzieleghem and Emilia Maria Becue had the following children:

+740 i. Paula Irène Vanzieleghem.
+741 ii. Robert Gérard Vanzieleghem.
+742 iii. Jacqueline Vanzieleghem.

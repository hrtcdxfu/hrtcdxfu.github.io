EIGHTH GENERATION

529. Carolus Ludovicus Vansieleghem was born on 8 Mar 1828 in Leffinge. He died on 14 Aug 1901 in Zandvoorde. He was mijn betovergroorvader.

He was married to Paulina Bertina Keters (daughter of Petrus Jacobus Keters and Rosalia Pattijn) on 7 Sep 1862 in Stene. Paulina Bertina Keters was born on 29 Sep 1834 in Zandvoorde. She died on 2 Sep 1909 in Zandvoorde. Carolus Ludovicus Vansieleghem and Paulina Bertina Keters had the following children:

NINTH GENERATION

609. Desiré Vansieleghem was born on 8 Oct 1893 in Aartrijke. He died on 10 Apr 1964 in Middelkerke. He was a rijkswachter.

He was married to Irma Cool in 1920 in Torhout. Irma Cool was born in 1898 in Torhout. She died in 1925 in Pervijze. Desiré Vansieleghem and Irma Cool had the following children:

783 i. Laura Vansieleghem was born on 18 Jan 1922 in Maaseik. She died on 18 Aug 1940 in Klerken.
+784 ii. Marie José Vansieleghem.

He was married to Elisa Maria Vanhoorne in 1926 in Pervijze. Elisa Maria Vanhoorne was born on 3 Aug 1903 in Pervijze. She died on 23 May 1978 in Oostende. Desiré Vansieleghem and Elisa Maria Vanhoorne had the following children:

+785 i. Blanche Vansieleghem.
+786 ii. Freddy Victor Vansieleghem.

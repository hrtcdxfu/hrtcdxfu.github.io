SEVENTH GENERATION

366. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem was born on 28 Oct 1781 in Leffinge. He died on 6 Jun 1811 in Slijpe.

He was married to Felicitas Colleta Vandenberghe on 29 Jan 1809 in Leffinge. Felicitas Colleta Vandenberghe was born on 9 May 1784 in Leffinge. She was born on 7 Jun 1784 in Leffinge. She died on 24 Aug 1869 in Slijpe. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem and Felicitas Colleta Vandenberghe had the following children:

+500 i. Maria Theresa Vansieleghem.
+501 ii. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem.
502 iii. Theresia Vansieleghem died on 25 Dec 1811 in Slijpe.

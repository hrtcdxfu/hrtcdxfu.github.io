THIRD GENERATION

54. Judocus Vansieleghem was born on 2 Mar 1674 in Lichtervelde. He died on 24 Apr 1727 in Ichtegem. He was st.v.g. RA brugge archief Lichtervelde 355 on 13 Dec 1737 in Lichtervelde. Woonde in 1716 in "De Somme" op de markt te Ichtegem.

FIFTH GENERATION

151. Petrus Antonius Vansieleghem was born on 9 Apr 1755 in Lichtervelde. He died on 9 Oct 1820 in Lichtervelde. He was a landlord-weaver-shopowner. getuigen: Petrus Jacobus Ramout & Judoca Vereenooghe

He was married to Anna Catharina Vanackere on 4 Feb 1790 in Lichtervelde. Anna Catharina Vanackere was born on 31 Mar 1769 in Gits. She died on 18 Feb 1849 in Lichtervelde. She was a spinster. Petrus Antonius Vansieleghem and Anna Catharina Vanackere had the following children:

248 i. Joannes Baptiste Vanzieleghem was born on 9 May 1791 in Gits.
+249 ii. Michiel Vanzieleghem.
+250 iii. Petrus Vanzieleghem.

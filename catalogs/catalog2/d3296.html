FIFTH GENERATION

142. Jacobus Ignatius Van Zieleghem was born on 11 Dec 1734 in Zwevezele, Brugge District, W. Flanders, Belgium. He died after 1792 in Zwevezele. He has Ancestral File number 13L9-MJW.

He was married to Marie Anne Vansteelandt (daughter of Albertus Vansteelandt and Elisabeth Van Aelmeersch) on 1 Feb 1759 in Zwevezele. Marie Anne Vansteelandt was born on 14 Feb 1734/35 in Zwevezele. She died on 18 Dec 1780 in Zwevezele. Jacobus Ignatius Van Zieleghem and Marie Anne Vansteelandt had the following children:

201 i. Emannuel Vanzieleghem was born on 2 Jan 1760 in Zwevezele.
202 ii. Isabella Vanzieleghem was born on 26 Sep 1762 in Zwevezele. She died on 1 Jan 1765 in Zwevezele.
+203 iii. Laventius Vanzieleghem.
204 iv. Laurentius Vanzieleghem was born on 18 Jan 1765 in Zwevezele.
205 v. Augustinus Vanzieleghem was born on 17 Oct 1767 in Zwevezele. He died on 4 Jun 1770 in Zwevezele.
+206 vi. Veronica Vanzieleghem.
+207 vii. Barbara Vanzieleghem.
208 viii. Joannes Ignatius Vanzieleghem was born on 29 Apr 1775 in Zwevezele.

He was married to Jacoba Gelande on 29 May 1781 in Zwevezele. Jacoba Gelande was born in Tielt. Jacobus Ignatius Van Zieleghem and Jacoba Gelande had the following children:

209 i. Francisca Vanzieleghem was born on 11 Sep 1781 in Zwevezele. She died on 6 Jan 1795 in Zwevezele.
210 ii. Franciscus Vanzieleghem was born on 13 Feb 1785 in Zwevezele. He died on 12 Nov 1785 in Zwevezele.
+211 iii. Amandus Vanzieleghem.
+212 iv. Petrus Vanzieleghem.
213 v. Ludovicus Vanzieleghem was born on 9 Jan 1790 in Zwevezele.
214 vi. Theresia Vanzieleghem was born on 21 Oct 1792 in Zwevezele. She died on 3 Jun 1794 in Zwevezele.

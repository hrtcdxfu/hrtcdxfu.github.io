NINTH GENERATION

608. Eugeen Vansieleghem was born on 16 Jan 1892 in Aartrijke. He died on 20 Dec 1969 in Koekelare. He was a farmer.

He was married to Eugenia Kerckhove in 1920 in Torhout. Eugenia Kerckhove was born in 1888 in Torhout. She died in 1979 in Koekelare. Eugeen Vansieleghem and Eugenia Kerckhove had the following children:

+778 i. Frans Joseph Vansieleghem.
+779 ii. Agnes Vansieleghem.
+780 iii. Alphère Vansieleghem.
+781 iv. Maria Andrea José Vansieleghem.
+782 v. Denise Vansieleghem.

EIGHTH GENERATION

422. Gustavus Julianus Vansieleghem was born on 2 Feb 1869 in Eernegem. He died on 19 Jan 1949 in Oostende. He was a kleermaker.

He was married to Leonie Julie Symaeys (daughter of Franciscus Ludovicus Symaeys and Amelia Sophia Lingier) on 11 May 1900. Leonie Julie Symaeys was born on 25 Jul 1878 in Eernegem. Gustavus Julianus Vansieleghem and Leonie Julie Symaeys had the following children:

582 i. Margareta Vansieleghem was born on 29 Sep 1901 in Eernegem. She died on 11 Feb 1983 in Brugge.

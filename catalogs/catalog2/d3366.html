SIXTH GENERATION

212. Petrus Vanzieleghem was born on 7 Jun 1783 in Zwevezele. He died on 11 Jan 1833 in Zwevezele.

He was married to Anna Dela Mellieure on 13 Nov 1812 in Wingene. Anna Dela Mellieure was born on 24 Mar 1784 in Zwevezele. She died on 3 Dec 1845 in Zwevezele. Petrus Vanzieleghem and Anna Dela Mellieure had the following children:

+308 i. Hypoliet Vanzieleghem.
309 ii. Juliana Vanzieleghem was born on 19 Jun 1822 in Zwevezele. She died on 18 Jul 1835 in Zwevezele.
310 iii. Felix Vanzieleghem was born on 23 Aug 1824 in Zwevezele. He died on 5 Sep 1826 in Zwevezele.
311 iv. Henricus Vanzieleghem was born on 25 Feb 1827 in Zwevezele. He died on 2 Sep 1828 in Zwevezele.
+312 v. Mathilda Vanzieleghem.

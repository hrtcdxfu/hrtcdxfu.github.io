SEVENTH GENERATION

355. Henricus Vanzieleghem was born on 11 Apr 1838 in Lichtervelde. He died on 26 Jan 1888 in Zwevezele. He was a brievenbesteller.

He was married to Lucie Maes on 27 Apr 1870 in Lichtervelde. Lucie Maes was born on 3 Oct 1849 in Lichtervelde. She died on 23 Sep 1886 in Zwevezele. Henricus Vanzieleghem and Lucie Maes had the following children:

+484 i. Celina Maria Vanzieleghem.
+485 ii. Juliana Maria Vanzieleghem.
486 iii. Augusta Maria Vanzieleghem was born on 4 Feb 1875 in Lichtervelde. She died on 14 Sep 1959 in Brugge.
487 iv. Julia Maria Vanzieleghem was born on 19 Nov 1876 in Zwevezele. She died on 25 Oct 1955 in Sint Amandsberg. She was a kloosterzuster.
+488 v. Mauritius Frederic Emiel Vanzieleghem.
489 vi. Omer Vanzieleghem was born on 4 Nov 1880 in Zwevezele. He died on 6 Feb 1882 in Zwevezele.
490 vii. Gossena Vanzieleghem was born on 7 Oct 1882 in Zwevezele. She died on 16 Nov 1883 in Zwevezele.
491 viii. Allina Vanzieleghem was born on 14 Oct 1883 in Zwevezele. She died on 17 Feb 1886 in Zwevezele.
492 ix. Polydor Oscar Vanzieleghem was born on 16 Sep 1886 in Zwevezele. He died on 24 Jan 1887 in Zwevezele.

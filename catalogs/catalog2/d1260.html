EIGHTH GENERATION

436. Henricus Josephus Vansieleghem was born on 25 Dec 1870 in Torhout. He died on 20 Jul 1932 in Oostende. He was a farmer.

He was married to Elisa Maria Rambour in 1896 in Torhout. Elisa Maria Rambour was born on 16 Jan 1876 in Torhout. Henricus Josephus Vansieleghem and Elisa Maria Rambour had the following children:

+621 i. Alicia Maria Josepha Vansieleghem.
622 ii. Mauritius Eugenius Ludovicus Vansieleghem was born in Apr 1898 in Oostende. He died on 19 Sep 1898 in Eernegem.

He was married to Zoé Maria Deltour in Lauwe. Zoé Maria Deltour was born on 4 Dec 1876 in Lauwe. She died on 6 Mar 1960 in Oostende. Henricus Josephus Vansieleghem and Zoé Maria Deltour had the following children:

+623 i. Anais Maria Ludovica Vansieleghem.

THIRD GENERATION

41. Joanna Vansieleghem was born on 13 Mar 1660 in Lichtervelde. She was Rijksarchief Brugge archief Lichtervelde nr.666 08/05/1694 st.v.g.. She was 4 kinderen.

She was married to Joannnes De Pape on 17 Feb 1680 in Lichtervelde. Joannnes De Pape died on 1 Dec 1693 in Lichtervelde.

TENTH GENERATION

936. Michel Aimé Joseph Vansieleghem was born on 19 Jan 1925 in Bredene. He died on 9 Sep 1996 in Oostende. He was a farmer.

He was married to Jeanne Georgette Vermoere (daughter of Carolus Ludovicus Vermoere and Elvira Maria Vanhee) on 3 Oct 1950 in Eernegem. Jeanne Georgette Vermoere was born on 19 Apr 1930 in Eernegem. She died on 22 Jul 2000 in Oostende. Michel Aimé Joseph Vansieleghem and Jeanne Georgette Vermoere had the following children:

+1172 i. Georges Camiel Vansieleghem.
+1173 ii. Monique Simonne Vansieleghem.
+1174 iii. Roger Maurice Vansieleghem.
1175 iv. Maurice André Vansieleghem was born on 30 Jan 1956 in Oostende. He died on 24 Nov 1974 in Oostende. He was an arbeider. He was verongelukt met motor.

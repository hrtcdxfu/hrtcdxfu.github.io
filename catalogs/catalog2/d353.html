NINTH GENERATION

704. Louis August Vansieleghem was born on 25 May 1865 in Leffinge. He died on 22 Apr 1937 in Leffinge. He was a stalmelker.

He was married to Rosalie Lecot on 8 Apr 1899 in Leffinge. Rosalie Lecot was born on 1 Jan 1875 in Leffinge. She died on 7 Jul 1954 in Zevekote. Louis August Vansieleghem and Rosalie Lecot had the following children:

+905 i. Raymond Vansieleghem.
+906 ii. Maria Vansieleghem.
907 iii. Carolus Augustus Vansieleghem was born on 9 Oct 1916 in Middelkerke. He died on 12 Mar 1918 in Oostende.

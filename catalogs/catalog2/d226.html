EIGHTH GENERATION

501. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem was born on 24 Oct 1810 in Slijpe. He was a mayor;farmer between 1856 and 1872 in Slijpe. He died on 8 Dec 1886 in Leffinge.

He was married to Suzanna Victoria Sanders (daughter of Petrus Jacobus Sanders and Anna Theresia Louagie) on 24 Jul 1839 in Slijpe. Suzanna Victoria Sanders was born on 26 Feb 1813 in Westende. She died on 4 May 1887 in Leffinge. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem and Suzanna Victoria Sanders had the following children:

680 i. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem died on 9 Sep 1840 in Slijpe.
681 ii. Rosalia Philipina Vansieleghem died on 27 Aug 1843 in Slijpe.
+682 iii. Joannes Franciscus Vansieleghem.
+683 iv. Philomena Sophia Vansieleghem.

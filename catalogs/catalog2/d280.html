TENTH GENERATION

895. Judith Marie Vansieleghem was born on 15 May 1898 in Wilskerke. She died on 16 Jan 1978 in Westende.

She was married to Lievinus Henricus Tratsaert on 27 May 1922 in Wilskerke. Lievinus Henricus Tratsaert was born on 27 Oct 1895 in Mannekesvere. He died on 26 Jul 1980 in Oostende. Judith Marie Vansieleghem and Lievinus Henricus Tratsaert had the following children:

+1120 i. Gustaaf Henri Tratsaert.
+1121 ii. Georges Charles Tratsaert.
+1122 iii. Lilian Genoveva Tratsaert.

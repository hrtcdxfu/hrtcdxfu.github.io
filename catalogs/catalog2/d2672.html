EIGHTH GENERATION

488. Mauritius Frederic Emiel Vanzieleghem was born on 27 Sep 1878 in Zwevezele. He died on 23 Jun 1951 in Ukkel. He was a timmerman.

He was married to Augusta Coletta Stroom on 29 Oct 1904 in Brussel. Augusta Coletta Stroom was born on 6 Oct 1884 in Booitshoeke. She died on 26 May 1953 in Beuzingen. Mauritius Frederic Emiel Vanzieleghem and Augusta Coletta Stroom had the following children:

+650 i. Juliana Lucie Vanzieleghem.
+651 ii. Charles Henri Vanzieleghem.

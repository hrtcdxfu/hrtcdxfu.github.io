SIXTH GENERATION

239. Carolus Vansieleghem was born on 3 Mar 1789 in Lichtervelde. He died on 20 Jun 1845 in Torhout. He was a landman wever.

He was married to Françoise Meseure on 9 Jun 1813 in Torhout. Françoise Meseure was born in 1791 in Lichtervelde. Carolus Vansieleghem and Françoise Meseure had the following children:

+325 i. Barbara Vansieleghem.
326 ii. Johannes Vansieleghem was born on 10 Nov 1815 in Lichtervelde. He died on 19 Nov 1815 in Lichtervelde.
327 iii. Rosalie Vansieleghem was born on 29 Oct 1816 in Lichtervelde.
328 iv. Jacobus Vansieleghem was born on 15 Dec 1817 in Lichtervelde.
+329 v. Joannes Vansieleghem.
330 vi. Isabella Vansieleghem was born on 7 Sep 1820 in Lichtervelde. She died on 26 May 1822 in Lichtervelde.
331 vii. Maria Ludovica Vansieleghem was born on 23 Sep 1821 in Lichtervelde.
332 viii. Ludovica Vansieleghem was born on 2 Nov 1822 in Lichtervelde. She died on 29 Jan 1823 in Lichtervelde.
333 ix. Ludovicus Vansieleghem was born on 24 May 1824 in Lichtervelde.
334 x. Eduardus Vansieleghem was born on 30 Jun 1825 in Lichtervelde. He died on 10 Jul 1825 in Lichtervelde.
335 xi. Constant Vansieleghem was born on 12 Apr 1827 in Torhout.

EIGHTH GENERATION

534. Justina Carolina Verhulst was born on 3 Jun 1834 in Leffinge. She died on 12 Dec 1922 in Leffinge.

She was married to Henricus Josephus Leopold Boutens (son of Franciscus Antonius Boutens and Catherina Sylvina Faict) on 23 Nov 1859 in Leffinge. Henricus Josephus Leopold Boutens was born on 18 Feb 1833 in Bredene. He died on 24 Dec 1885 in Leffinge.

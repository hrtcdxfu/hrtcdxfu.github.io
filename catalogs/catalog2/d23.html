SECOND GENERATION

2. Joannes Van sieleghem was born in 1631 in Lichtervelde. He was boedelbeschrijving n° 819 on 12 Nov 1707 in Lichtervelde. He died on 12 Jan 1730 in Lichtervelde. He was an Officer 3° class.

He was married to Judoca Desmedt on 7 Sep 1642 in Lichtervelde. Judoca Desmedt died on 10 Feb 1682 in Lichtervelde. Joannes Van sieleghem and Judoca Desmedt had the following children:

10 i. Petronella Vansieleghem was born on 19 Jul 1643 in Lichtervelde.
11 ii. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 30 Sep 1645 in Lichtervelde. He was born on 30 Nov 1645 in Lichtervelde.
+12 iii. Michael Vansieleghem.
+13 iv. Judocus Vansieleghem.
14 v. Maria Vansieleghem was born on 24 Dec 1653 in Lichtervelde. She died in 1654 in Lichtervelde.
15 vi. Maria Vansieleghem was born on 4 Oct 1655 in Lichtervelde. She was geboren on 9 Oct 1655.
16 vii. Jacoba Vansieleghem was born on 4 Apr 1660 in Lichtervelde.
17 viii. Anna Vansieleghem was born on 31 Oct 1662 in Lichtervelde.
18 ix. Joanna Vansieleghem was born on 29 Jun 1666 in Lichtervelde.
+19 x. Jacob Vansieleghem.
+20 xi. Petrus Vansieleghem.

He was married to Joanna Vande Kinderen on 17 Feb 1669 in Lichtervelde. Joanna Vande Kinderen died on 14 Mar 1707 in Lichtervelde. Joannes Van sieleghem and Joanna Vande Kinderen had the following children:

+21 i. Joannes Vansieleghem.
22 ii. Jacobus Van sieleghem was born in 1672 in Lichtervelde. He (or she) died after 1730 in Zedelgem.
23 iii. Catharina Vansieleghem was born on 12 Jan 1674 in Lichtervelde. She died before 1707.
24 iv. Josephus Vansieleghem was born on 20 Jan 1677 in Lichtervelde.
+25 v. Marinus Vansieleghem.
+26 vi. Michiel Adriaan Vansieleghem.

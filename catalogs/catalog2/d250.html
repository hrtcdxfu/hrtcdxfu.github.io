EIGHTH GENERATION

527. Eduardus Charles Vansieleghem was born on 23 Apr 1822 in Leffinge. He died on 29 Jun 1886 in Leffinge.

He was married to Theresia Catharina Souvagie (daughter of Jacobus Josephus Souvagie and Joanna Declercq) on 1 May 1854 in Leffinge. Theresia Catharina Souvagie was born on 20 Feb 1831 in Leffinge. She died on 13 Jun 1894 in Leffinge. Eduardus Charles Vansieleghem and Theresia Catharina Souvagie had the following children:

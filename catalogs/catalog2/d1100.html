TENTH GENERATION

780. Alphère Vansieleghem was born on 18 Sep 1926 in Wingene. He died on 30 Oct 1994 in Brugge. He was a farmer.

He was married to Agnes Huyghe in 1951 in Bovekerke. Alphère Vansieleghem and Agnes Huyghe had the following children:

+1027 i. Arnold Henri Vansieleghem.
+1028 ii. Marie Ann Juliette Henriette Vansieleghem.
1029 iii. Hugo Jules Vansieleghem (Private).
+1030 iv. Anneke Gerda Francine Vansieleghem.

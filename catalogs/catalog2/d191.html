FIFTH GENERATION

176. Joanna Clara Vansieleghem was born on 26 Dec 1734 in Ichtegem.

She was married to Alexander Henri Hallevoet on 22 Jun 1753 in Brugge. Joanna Clara Vansieleghem and Alexander Henri Hallevoet had the following children:

+265 i. Isabella Hallevoet.
266 ii. Pieter Hallevoet was born on 3 Feb 1766 in Ichtegem. He died on 21 Jan 1840 in Ichtegem. He was a brouwer. Baatte "De Somme" te Ichtegem uit van 1806 tot 1840.
+267 iii. Balbina Hallevoet.

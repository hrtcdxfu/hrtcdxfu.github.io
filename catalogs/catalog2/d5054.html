EIGHTH GENERATION

500. Maria Theresa Vansieleghem was born on 6 Dec 1809 in Slijpe. She died on 31 Dec 1837 in Slijpe. She was a farmer.

She was married to Engelbert Missuwe (son of Petrus Missuwe and Maria Johanna Declerck) on 7 Mar 1837 in Slijpe. Engelbert Missuwe was born on 6 Aug 1799 in Slijpe. He was a farmer.

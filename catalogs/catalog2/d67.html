THIRD GENERATION

51. Clara Vansieleghem was born on 19 Nov 1666 in Lichtervelde. She died on 18 Oct 1747 in Torhout.

Joannes Kesteloot (son of Jacobus Kesteloot and Anna Verhoye) was born on 17 Apr 1670 in Torhout. He died on 26 Nov 1694 in Torhout. Clara Vansieleghem and Joannes Kesteloot had the following children:

102 i. Anna Maria Kesteloot was born on 25 Oct 1687 in Lichtervelde.
103 ii. Maria Magdalena Kesteloot was born on 22 Aug 1689 in Lichtervelde. She died before 1691.
104 iii. Maria Josephia Kesteloot was born on 22 Aug 1691 in Lichtervelde.
105 iv. Petronella Kesteloot was born on 28 Apr 1694 in Lichtervelde. She was 2 kinderen.

She was married to Guillaume Stroybant on 24 May 1701 in Kortemark. Guillaume Stroybant died on 18 Aug 1721 in Torhout. Clara Vansieleghem and Guillaume Stroybant had the following children:

106 i. Maria Suzanna Stroobant was born in 1701 or 1702 in Kortemark.
107 ii. Mauritius Stroobant was born in 1703.
108 iii. Theresia Victoria Stroybant was born on 23 Dec 1705 in Kortemark.
109 iv. Josephus Stroybant was born on 9 Jun 1709 in Torhout.

THIRD GENERATION

9. Carolus Van Rolleghem was born on 3 Jan 1698 in Ardooie. He died on 23 Jun 1741 in Ardooie.

He was married to Anna Birgitta Rubrez on 24 Jan 1723 in Ardooie. Anna Birgitta Rubrez was born on 13 Sep 1700 in Ardooie. She died on 28 Nov 1747 in Ardooie.

FIFTH GENERATION

31. Emanuel Vansieleghem was born on 17 Jul 1746 in Ardooie. He died on 13 Dec 1813 in Ardooie. He was a dienstknecht. getuigen: Leonard Vansieleghem & Catharina Hostens

He was married to Rosalie Hoste on 23 Feb 1791 in Ardooie. Rosalie Hoste was born in 1772 in Koolskamp. She died on 28 Feb 1846 in Ardooie. She was a spinster. Emanuel Vansieleghem and Rosalie Hoste had the following children:

37 i. David Vansieleghem was born on 17 Dec 1791 in Ardooie.
38 ii. Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 25 Aug 1795 in Ardooie. She died on 18 Jul 1827 in Koolskamp.
39 iii. Anna Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 17 Mar 1794 in Ardooie. She died on 6 Jun 1795 in Ardooie.

EIGHTH GENERATION

62. Cyriel Vanzieleghem was born on 3 Oct 1873 in Beveren-Roeselare. He died on 9 Nov 1942 in Gent.

He was married to Augusta Elisabeth Bailleu in 1901 in Veurne. Augusta Elisabeth Bailleu was born on 29 May 1863 in Veurne. She died on 13 Mar 1934 in Roeselare. Cyriel Vanzieleghem and Augusta Elisabeth Bailleu had the following children:

+77 i. Helena Maria Germana Vanzieleghem.
+78 ii. Julia Helena Vanzieleghem.
+79 iii. Maria Louise Apollonia Vanzieleghem.

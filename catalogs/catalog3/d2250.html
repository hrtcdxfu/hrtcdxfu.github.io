FOURTH GENERATION

17. Emanuel Vansieleghem was born on 9 Mar 1715 in Ardooie. getuigen: Martha Tienpont & Brigitta Van Nieuwenhuyse

He was married to Anna Catharina van Nieuwenhuyse (daughter of Ludovicus van Nieuwenhuyse and Anna Maria Devos) on 30 Jan 1745 in Ardooie. Anna Catharina van Nieuwenhuyse was born on 4 Jun 1713 in Ardooie. Emanuel Vansieleghem and Anna Catharina van Nieuwenhuyse had the following children:

+31 i. Emanuel Vansieleghem.
32 ii. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 25 Aug 1749 in Ardooie.
33 iii. Petrus Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 31 Jan 1752 in Ardooie. He died on 4 Apr 1779 in Ardooie.
34 iv. Hubrecht Vansieleghem was born in Ardooie.

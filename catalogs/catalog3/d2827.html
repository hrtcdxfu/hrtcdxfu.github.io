EIGHTH GENERATION

64. Alfons Vanzieleghem was born on 19 Jan 1877 in Beveren-Roeselare. He died on 30 Oct 1959 in Meulebeke. He was a kleermaker.

Urbanie Lucia Degrendele was born on 3 Oct 1880 in Roeselare. She died on 25 Sep 1954 in Tielt. Alfons Vanzieleghem and Urbanie Lucia Degrendele had the following children:

+86 i. Maurice Vanzieleghem.
+87 ii. Paula Estella Vanzieleghem.

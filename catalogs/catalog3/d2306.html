NINTH GENERATION

67. Leonia Maria Vanzieleghem was born on 16 Feb 1896 in Tielt. She died on 2 Sep 1981 in Pittem.

She was married to Victor Verrewaere (son of Karel Lodewijk Verrewaere and Ludovica Naert) on 29 Sep 1920 in Pittem. Victor Verrewaere was born on 18 Mar 1895 in Egem. He died on 24 Oct 1979 in Egem.

SIXTH GENERATION

40. Ivo Vansieleghem was born on 4 Jan 1797 in Ardooie. He died on 30 Jan 1865 in Koolskamp.

He was married to Amelia Coussée (daughter of Franciscus Coussée and Anna Vandekerckhove) on 3 Feb 1831 in Koolskamp. Amelia Coussée was born on 19 Jul 1800 in Egem. She was born 19 Missidor (juli) VII in Egem. She was a spinster. Ivo Vansieleghem and Amelia Coussée had the following children:

46 i. Virginie Vansieleghem was born in 1831 in Bulskamp. She died on 28 Aug 1832 in Koolskamp.
+47 ii. Constant Vansieleghem.
48 iii. Romanie Marie Vansieleghem was born on 4 Oct 1836 in Koolskamp.
49 iv. Nathalie Vansieleghem was born on 11 Aug 1837 in Koolskamp.
50 v. Marie Thérèse Vansieleghem was born on 26 Dec 1838 in Koolskamp.
51 vi. Eugenia Vansieleghem was born on 8 Jul 1840 in Koolskamp.
52 vii. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 28 May 1841 in Koolskamp.

THIRD GENERATION

5. Adrianus Vansieleghem was born on 30 Oct 1658 in Ardooie. He died on 3 Apr 1744 in Ardooie.

He was married to Petronella Casteleyn on 27 Sep 1700 in Ardooie. Petronella Casteleyn was born on 27 May 1663 in Ardooie. She died on 10 Jan 1754 in Ardooie. Adrianus Vansieleghem and Petronella Casteleyn had the following children:

+12 i. Judocus Vansieleghem.
13 ii. Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 28 Jan 1705 in Ardooie.
+14 iii. Anna Maria Vansieleghem.
+15 iv. Adrianus Vansieleghem.
16 v. Pieter Vansieleghem was born on 8 May 1714 in Ardooie. He died on 5 Oct 1714 in Ardooie.
+17 vi. Emanuel Vansieleghem.
18 vii. Anna Theresia Vansieleghem was born on 9 Mar 1715 in Ardooie. She died on 1 Nov 1778 in Ardooie.
19 viii. Hubertus Vansieleghem was born on 7 Mar 1717 in Ardooie. He died on 31 Oct 1790 in Ardooie.
20 ix. Petrus Joannes Vansieleghem was born on 8 May 1719 in Ardooie. He died on 5 Oct 1719 in Ardooie.
21 x. Petrus Josephus Vansieleghem was born on 22 Mar 1721 in Ardooie. He died on 6 Jan 1802 in Ardooie.
+22 xi. Ignatius Vansieleghem.

He was married to Marie Venant. Marie Venant died on 3 Dec 1712 in Ardooie.

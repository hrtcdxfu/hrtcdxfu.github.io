EIGHTH GENERATION

63. Odiel Vanzieleghem was born on 21 Dec 1874 in Beveren. He died on 3 Jan 1940 in Roeselare.

He was married to Maria Vanrobays. Maria Vanrobays was born on 1 Dec 1876 in Ingelmunster. She died on 2 Jan 1948 in Roeselare. Odiel Vanzieleghem and Maria Vanrobays had the following children:

+80 i. August M. Vanzieleghem.
+81 ii. Marcel Vanzieleghem.
+82 iii. Camiel Vanzieleghem.
+83 iv. Victor Gustaaf Vanzieleghem.
+84 v. Maria Mathilde Vanzieleghem.
+85 vi. Martha Erna Vanzieleghem.

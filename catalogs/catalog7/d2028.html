FOURTH GENERATION

5. Catharina Vansieleghem was born on 17 Jun 1661 in Ardooie. She died on 18 Aug 1695 in Ardooie.

She was married to Judocus Callewaert (son of Jan Callewaert and Judoca De Raedt) on 20 Jun 1682 in Ardooie. Judocus Callewaert was born on 18 Apr 1657 in Pittem. He died on 8 Nov 1729 in Ardooie. Catharina Vansieleghem and Judocus Callewaert had the following children:

8 i. Joannes Callewaert was born on 18 Jul 1683 in Ardooie. He died after 1696.
9 ii. Catharina Callewaert was born on 19 Jan 1685 in Ardooie. She died before 1696.
10 iii. Jossyne Callewaert was born on 4 Mar 1686 in Ardooie. She died after 1696.
11 iv. Elisabeth Callewaert was born on 21 Aug 1687 in Ardooie. She died before 1696.
12 v. Joanna Callewaert was born on 9 Feb 1689 in Ardooie. She died before 1696.
13 vi. Judocus Callewaert was born on 11 Jun 1691 in Ardooie. He died on 15 May 1782 in Lichtervelde.
14 vii. Maria Catharina Callewaert was born on 30 Apr 1693 in Ardooie. She died before 1696.
15 viii. Jacobus Callewaert was born on 7 Oct 1694 in Ardooie. He died after Feb 1756.

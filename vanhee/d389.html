SIXTH GENERATION

25. Jacobus Van Hee was born in 1689. He died on 26 Sep 1764 in Eernegem.

He was married to Joanna Cornelia De Bouvere (daughter of Pieter De Bouvere and Catherina Rotsaert) on 21 Jan 1735 in Eernegem. Joanna Cornelia De Bouvere died on 8 Nov 1785 in Eernegem. Jacobus Van Hee and Joanna Cornelia De Bouvere had the following children:

45 i. Joannes Philippus Jacobus Van Hee was born on 1 May 1736 in Eernegem. He died on 27 Jun 1736 in Eernegem.
+46 ii. Maria Joanna Van Hee.
+47 iii. Petrus Joannes Van Hee.
+48 iv. Regina Josepha Van Hee.
49 v. Guilielmus Van Hee was born in 1742 in Eernegem. He died on 23 Oct 1754.
50 vi. Godeliva Catharina Van Hee was born on 5 Aug 1744. She died on 18 Mar 1747 in Eernegem.
51 vii. Regina Josepha Van Hee was born on 17 Feb 1747 in Eernegem. She died in 1818 in Eernegem.
52 viii. Jacobus Bernardus Van Hee was born on 17 Nov 1749 in Eernegem. He died on 25 Jan 1751 in Eernegem.
+53 ix. Jacobus Joannes Van Hee.
54 x. Jancentius Franciscus Van Hee was born on 5 Sep 1754 in Eernegem.
55 xi. Joanna Baptista Van Hee was born on 5 Jan 1760 in Zaffelare.

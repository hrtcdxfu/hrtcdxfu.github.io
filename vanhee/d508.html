EIGHTH GENERATION

80. Jacobus Van Hee was born on 2 Oct 1772 in Eernegem.

He was married to Isabella Clara Aernout (daughter of Pieter Aernout and Godelieve Genoveva Inghelbrecht). Isabella Clara Aernout was born on 21 Jan 1774 in Eernegem. She died on 14 Apr 1846 in Eernegem.

NINTH GENERATION

121. Petrus Jacobus Vanhee (1) was born on 16 Dec 1795 in Koekelare. He was baptised on 16 Dec 1795 in Koekelare. He died on 11 Apr 1859 in Eernegem.

He was married to Sophia Beuselinck on 19 Jan 1826 in Eernegem. Sophia Beuselinck(1) was born on 8 Apr 1801 in Eernegem. She died on 28 May 1870 in Eernegem. Petrus Jacobus Vanhee and Sophia Beuselinck had the following children:

130 i. Pieter Franciscus Vanhee was born on 30 Dec 1826. He died on 30 Aug 1837 in Eernegem.
131 ii. Carolus Ludovicus van hee was born on 21 Mar 1828 in Eernegem. He died on 29 Dec 1861 in Eernegem. He was not married.
+132 iii. Franciscus Ludovicus Vanhee.
+133 iv. Emilie Louise Vanhee.

135 vi. Ludovicus Vanhee was born on 27 Jan 1836 in Eernegem. He died on 15 Apr 1859 in Eernegem.

56 i. Jacobus Van Massenhove was born in Jul 1733 in Eernegem. He died on 30 Jan 1743 in Eernegem. He was unmarried.
57 ii. Ludovicus Joannes Van Massenhove was born on 26 Sep 1736 in Eernegem. He died on 7 Nov 1786 in Eernegem.
58 iii. Maria Catharina Van Massenhove was born in 1734 in Eernegem.

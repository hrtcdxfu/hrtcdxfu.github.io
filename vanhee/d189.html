FOURTH GENERATION

4. Cornelius Van Ee (1) was born in 1598 in Eernegem. He died on 1 May 1667 in Eernegem. after 1600 namechanging to Van Hee

He was married to Joanna Soens (daughter of Soens) on 2 Oct 1621 in Eernegem. Joanna Soens died on 2 May 1626. She was born in Ichtegem. Cornelius Van Ee and Joanna Soens had the following children:

+5 i. Catherine Van Ee.
6 ii. Passcharius Van Ee was born on 1 May 1624 in Eernegem. He died on 21 Oct 1648 in Eernegem.

He was married to Joanna Van Waelscappel on 7 Feb 1627 in Oedelem. Joanna Van Waelscappel (1) was born in 1598. She died on 22 Nov 1678 in Eernegem. Cornelius Van Ee and Joanna Van Waelscappel had the following children:

+7 i. Joanna Van Ee.
8 ii. Cornelius Van Ee was born on 15 Nov 1629 in Eernegem.
+9 iii. Margaretha Van Ee.
+10 iv. Jacoba Van Ee.
+11 v. Cornelius Van Ee.

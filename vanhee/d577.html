TENTH GENERATION

132. Franciscus Ludovicus Vanhee was born on 8 Sep 1829 in Eernegem.

He was married to Louisa Logghe (daughter of Pieter Logghe and Francisca Dugardeyn) on 10 Jul 1874 in Jabbeke. Louisa Logghe was born on 3 Apr 1847 in Jabbeke. Franciscus Ludovicus Vanhee and Louisa Logghe had the following children:

142 i. Camilius Augustus Vanhee was born on 5 Nov 1880 in Zerkegem. He died on 14 Nov 1880 in Zerkegem.
143 ii. Gustavus Joannes Vanhee was born on 26 Nov 1831 in Eernegem.

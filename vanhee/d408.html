SEVENTH GENERATION

48. Regina Josepha Van Hee was born on 22 Feb 1740 in Eernegem.

She was married to Leonardus Jonckheere on 15 Jul 1768 in Eernegem. Leonardus Jonckheere died on 25 Feb 1818 in Eernegem. Regina Josepha Van Hee and Leonardus Jonckheere had the following children:

90 i. Maria Jonckheere was born in 1769.
91 ii. Maria Jonckheere was born in 1771.

She was married to Cornelis Knudde on 3 Feb 1774. Regina Josepha Van Hee and Cornelis Knudde had the following children:

92 i. Godelieva Catherina Knudde was born on 28 Aug 1778 in Eernegem.
93 ii. Sophia Knudde.

FIFTH GENERATION

10. Jacoba Van Ee was born on 19 Sep 1634 in Eernegem. She died on 11 May 1691 in Eernegem.

She was married to Joannes De Vogelaere on 30 Apr 1658 in Eernegem. Jacoba Van Ee and Joannes De Vogelaere had the following children:

17 i. Jacob Voghelaere was born in 1660.
18 ii. Jan Voghelaere was born in 1661.
19 iii. Pieter Voghelaere was born in 1666.
20 iv. Anna Voghelaere was born in 1676.

She was married to Adrianus Dufour on 10 Mar 1687 in Eernegem.

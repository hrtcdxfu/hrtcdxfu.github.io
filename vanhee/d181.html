EIGHTH GENERATION

84. Petrus Jacobus Van Hee (1) was born on 14 Sep 1759 in Eernegem. He was baptised on 14 Sep 1759 in Eernegem. He died on 4 Apr 1825 in Eernegem. He was a farmer.

He was married to Isabelle Claire De Reeper (daughter of Joannes De Reeper and Eugenie Vandendriessche) on 10 Aug 1802 in Koekelare. Isabelle Claire De Reeper was born on 29 Mar 1784 in Koekelare. She died on 28 Nov 1843 in Eernegem. She was a farmer. Petrus Jacobus Van Hee and Isabelle Claire De Reeper had the following children:

106 ii. Colette Sophie Van Hee was born on 7 Jan 1805 in Eernegem.
107 iii. François Van Hee was born on 19 Nov 1806 in Eernegem.
108 iv. Louis Van Hee was born on 4 Apr 1808 in Eernegem.
109 v. Henri Joseph Van Hee was born on 4 Nov 1809 in Eernegem.
110 vi. Catherine Van Hee was born on 10 Mar 1811 in Eernegem.
111 vii. Jacobus Van Hee was born on 17 Jul 1813 in Eernegem.
112 viii. Isabelle Van Hee was born on 1 Aug 1815 in Eernegem.

115 xi. Amandus Franciscus Van Hee was born on 9 Sep 1820 in Eernegem.
116 xii. Joannes Van Hee was born on 18 Jul 1824 in Eernegem.

He was married to Rosalia Perdu (daughter of Ludovicus Perdu and Maria Cathatarina Decoster) on 15 Jan 1792 in Koekelare. Rosalia Perdu(1) was born on 26 Dec 1759 in Koekelare. She was baptised on 26 Dec 1759 in Koekelare. She died in 1797 in Koekelare. Petrus Jacobus Van Hee and Rosalia Perdu had the following children:

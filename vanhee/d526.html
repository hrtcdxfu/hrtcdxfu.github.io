EIGHTH GENERATION

62. Petronilla Godeliva Vanhee was born on 9 Sep 1745 in Eernegem.

She was married to Petrus Christoffel Tempelaere on 13 Aug 1771. Petrus Christoffel Tempelaere died on 16 Nov 1794 in Sint Michiels. Petronilla Godeliva Vanhee and Petrus Christoffel Tempelaere had the following children:

98 i. Marianne Tempelaere.
99 ii. Godelieve Tempelaere.
100 iii. Anna Theresia Tempelaere.
101 iv. Ludovicus Tempelaere.
102 v. Catherina Tempelaere.
103 vi. Pieter Tempelaere.

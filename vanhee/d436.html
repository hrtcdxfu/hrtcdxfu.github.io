EIGHTH GENERATION

96. Jacobus Van Hee was born on 6 Jun 1791 in Jabbeke. He was a farmer.

He was married to Anna Theresia Verplancke (daughter of Carolus Verplancke and Isabella Desnoeck) in 1820 in Jabbeke. Anna Theresia Verplancke was born on 26 Sep 1794 in Jabbeke. Jacobus Van Hee and Anna Theresia Verplancke had the following children:

+123 i. Theresia Vanhee.
124 ii. Antonius Franciscus Van Hee was born on 14 Jul 1825 in Jabbeke. He died on 25 Sep 1827 in Jabbeke.
125 iii. Ludovicus Vanhee was born on 23 May 1827 in Jabbeke. He (or she) died on 29 May 1846 in Jabbeke.
126 iv. Carolus Van hee was born on 4 May 1832 in Jabbeke. He was no children found.
127 v. Francisca Van Hee was born on 6 Apr 1834 in Jabbeke. She died on 16 Jul 1835 in Jabbeke.
+128 vi. Francisca Van hee.

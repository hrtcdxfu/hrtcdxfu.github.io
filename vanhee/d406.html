SEVENTH GENERATION

46. Maria Joanna Van Hee was born on 22 Sep 1737 in Eernegem. She died on 3 Jan 1801 in Snellegem.

She was married to Petrus Strubbe (son of Petrus Strubbe and Petronella Schoolmeesters) on 5 Jan 1760 in Snellegem. Petrus Strubbe was born on 26 Jul 1740 in Snellegem. He died on 21 Mar 1771 in Eernegem.

She was married to Joannes Vanrenterghem on 9 May 1775 in Snellegem.

She was married to Joachim DeKeyser in 1779 in Snellegem.

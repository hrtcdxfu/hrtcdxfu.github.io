SEVENTH GENERATION

42. Ludovicus Joannes Van Hee(1) was born on 18 May 1732 in Eernegem. He was baptised on 18 May 1732 in Eernegem. He died on 6 May 1804 in Eernegem.

He was married to Brigitte Van Renterghem (daughter of Joannes Van Renterghem and Brigitte Loontjes) on 25 Jun 1771 in Snellegem. Brigitte Van Renterghem was born in 1745 in Snellegem. She died on 17 Nov 1775 in Eernegem. Ludovicus Joannes Van Hee and Brigitte Van Renterghem had the following children:

+80 i. Jacobus Van Hee.
81 ii. Carolus Bernardus Van Hee was born on 27 Oct 1773 in Eernegem.
82 iii. Leonardus Franciscus Van Hee was born on 8 Dec 1774 in Eernegem.
83 iv. ? Van Hee died on 17 Nov 1775 in Eernegem. He (or she) was born on 17 Nov 1775 in Eernegem.

He was married to Angela De Jaeghere (daughter of Gilles De Jaeghere and Anna De Nys) on 31 May 1776 in Eernegem. Angela De Jaeghere was born in 1750. She died on 10 Feb 1792.

He was married to Maria Catharina Strubbe (daughter of Petrus Strubbe and Josepha Tempelaere) on 13 May 1758 in Snellegem. Maria Catharina Strubbe (1) was born on 24 Jul 1740 in Snellegem. She died on 21 Mar 1771 in Eernegem. Ludovicus Joannes Van Hee and Maria Catharina Strubbe had the following children:

+84 i. Petrus Jacobus Van Hee.
85 ii. Philippus Ludovicus Van Hee was born on 29 Sep 1761 in Eernegem.
86 iii. Regina Francisca Van Hee was born on 8 Mar 1763 in Eernegem.
87 iv. Anna Maria Van Hee was born on 4 Aug 1765 in Eernegem.
88 v. Joannes Bernardus Van Hee was born on 7 Jan 1768 in Eernegem.
89 vi. Joanna Clara Van Hee was born on 15 Dec 1769 in Eernegem.

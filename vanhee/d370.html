FIFTH GENERATION

9. Margaretha Van Ee was born on 22 Aug 1632 in Eernegem. She died on 6 Nov 1673 in Eernegem.

She was married to Petrus Lannoye on 2 Oct 1656 in Eernegem. Margaretha Van Ee and Petrus Lannoye had the following children:

12 i. Jacquemijnken Delanoye.
13 ii. Jacob Delanoye.
14 iii. Pieternelle Delanoye.
15 iv. Joosynken Delanoye.
16 v. Pieter Delanoye was born in 1661.

SIXTH GENERATION

23. Petrus (de oude) Vanhee (1) was born in 1681 in Eernegem. He died on 23 Jun 1759 in Eernegem.

He was married to Margaretha Claeys on 25 Feb 1710 in Eernegem. Margaretha Claeys died on 8 Jul 1716 in Eernegem. Petrus (de oude) Vanhee and Margaretha Claeys had the following children:

+29 i. Maria Van Hee.
+30 ii. Petrus (de jonge) Vanhee.
31 iii. ? Vanhee died on 21 Jun 1715 in Eernegem.
32 iv. Anna Vanhee was born on 29 May 1716 in Eernegem. She died on 30 May 1716.
33 v. Cecilia Van Hee died on 30 Jul 1726.

He was married to Catherina Verpoorte on 14 Oct 1742 in Eernegem. Petrus (de oude) Vanhee and Catherina Verpoorte had the following children:

34 i. Carolus Josephus Vanhee was born on 11 Dec 1746 in Eernegem. He died on 1 Oct 1769 in Eernegem.

He was married to Judoca Verhaeghe (daughter of Gelin Verhaeghe and Theresia Lauwers) on 26 Jan 1717 in Eernegem. Judoca Verhaeghe (1) was born on 3 Jan 1698 in Aartrijke. She was baptised on 3 Jan 1698 in Aartrijke. She died on 4 May 1739 in Eernegem. Petrus (de oude) Vanhee and Judoca Verhaeghe had the following children:

35 i. Cornelius Van Hee was born on 17 Jan 1718 in Eernegem. He died on 30 Jul 1724 in Eernegem.
36 ii. Jacoba Van Hee was born on 16 Nov 1719 in Eernegem. She died on 30 Oct 1731 in Eernegem.
37 iii. Christianus Van Hee was born on 3 Dec 1721 in Eernegem. He died on 26 Jul 1733 in Eernegem.
+38 iv. Emmerentina Van Hee.
+39 v. Godelieve Rosalia Van Hee.
40 vi. Angelina Van Hee was born on 16 Jan 1730 in Eernegem. She died on 5 Feb 1730 in Eernegem.
+41 vii. Isabella Clara Van Hee.
+42 viii. Ludovicus Joannes Van Hee.
+43 ix. Joannes Jacobus Van Hee.
44 x. Arnoldus Jacobus Van Hee was born on 22 Oct 1736 in Eernegem. He died on 31 Oct 1736 in Eernegem.

FIFTH GENERATION

11. Cornelius Van Ee (1) was born on 8 Jan 1637 in Eernegem. He was baptised on 8 Jan 1637 in Eernegem. He died on 31 May 1699 in Eernegem.

He was married to Joanna Haesaert (daughter of Gilles Haesaert) on 12 Nov 1662 in Eernegem. Joanna Haesaert died on 15 Dec 1675.

He was married to Petronilla Van Massenhove (daughter of Jacobus Van Massenhove and Maria Demeester) on 14 Jun 1676 in Eernegem. Petronilla Van Massenhove (1) was born on 20 Jul 1655 in Ichtegem. She was baptised on 20 Jul 1655 in Ichtegem. She died on 10 Oct 1728 in Eernegem. Cornelius Van Ee and Petronilla Van Massenhove had the following children:

+21 i. Jacmynthia Van Hee.
22 ii. Cornelius Van Hee was born on 26 Dec 1679 in Eernegem. probably not married

+23 iii. Petrus (de oude) Vanhee.
+24 iv. Christianus Adrianus Van Hee.
+25 v. Jacobus Van Hee.
+26 vi. Jacoba Van Hee.
+27 vii. Cornelius Van Hee.
28 viii. ?? Van Ee was born in 1699 in Eernegem. He (or she) died on 26 Sep 1699 in Eernegem.

EIGHTH GENERATION

94. Catherina Van Hee was born on 3 Jan 1786 in Jabbeke. She died on 27 Dec 1866 in Jabbeke.

She was married to Jacques Strubbe (son of Jacques Strubbe and Reine DeKetelaere) on 27 Mar 1818 in Jabbeke. Jacques Strubbe was born on 29 Jan 1775 in Varsenare. He died on 16 Sep 1854 in Jabbeke.

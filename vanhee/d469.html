SEVENTH GENERATION

39. Godelieve Rosalia Van Hee was born on 9 Aug 1727 in Eernegem. She died on 20 Aug 1782 in Eernegem.

She was married to Michaël Inghelbregt on 3 Feb 1745 in Eernegem. Michaël Inghelbregt was born on 5 Sep 1714 in Eernegem. He died on 2 Dec 1770 in Eernegem. Godelieve Rosalia Van Hee and Michaël Inghelbregt had the following children:

68 i. Bernardus Inghelbrecht was born in 1750.
+69 ii. Isabella Eugenie Clara Inghelbrecht.
70 iii. Cornelius Inghelbrecht was born in 1754.
+71 iv. Godelieve Genoveva Inghelbrecht.
72 v. Pieter Inghelbrecht was born in 1760.
73 vi. Steven Inghelbrecht was born in 1762.
74 vii. Jan Inghelbrecht was born in 1767.
75 viii. Michiel Inghelbrecht.
76 ix. Pieter Inghelbrecht.
77 x. Staphinus Inghelbrecht.
78 xi. Joannes Inghelbrecht.

She was married to Augustinus Peper on 27 Aug 1771 in Eernegem. Augustinus Peper was born on 9 Dec 1736 in Eernegem. He died on 20 Jun 1784 in Eernegem. Godelieve Rosalia Van Hee and Augustinus Peper had the following children:

79 i. Isabella Clara Peper was born on 3 Apr 1782 in Ichtegem.

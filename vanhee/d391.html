SIXTH GENERATION

27. Cornelius Van Hee was born on 29 May 1696 in Eernegem. He died on 11 Oct 1735 in Eernegem.

He was married to Godelieve Hendrickx on 18 Jul 1724 in Eernegem. Godelieve Hendrickx was born on 10 Mar 1707 in Eernegem. She died on 21 Apr 1766 in Eernegem.

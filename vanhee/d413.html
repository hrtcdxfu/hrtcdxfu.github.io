SEVENTH GENERATION

53. Jacobus Joannes Van Hee was born on 9 Jul 1752 in Eernegem.

He was married to Joanna Mostrey on 15 Nov 1784 in Jabbeke. Joanna Mostrey was born in Zerkegem. Jacobus Joannes Van Hee and Joanna Mostrey had the following children:

+94 i. Catherina Van Hee.
+95 ii. Anna Theresia Van Hee.
+96 iii. Jacobus Van Hee.

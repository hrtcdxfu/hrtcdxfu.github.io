NINTH GENERATION

123. Theresia Vanhee was born on 20 Jan 1822 in Jabbeke.

She was married to Josephus Philippus Van Hardenberg (son of Philippus Van Hardenberg and Anna Demeulemeester) in 1854 in Jabbeke. Josephus Philippus Van Hardenberg was born on 10 May 1827 in Brugge.

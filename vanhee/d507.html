NINTH GENERATION

104. Isabella Clara Aernout was born on 21 Jan 1774 in Eernegem. She died on 14 Apr 1846 in Eernegem.

She was married to Jacobus Van Hee (son of Ludovicus Joannes Van Hee and Brigitte Van Renterghem). Jacobus Van Hee was born on 2 Oct 1772 in Eernegem.

SEVENTH GENERATION

30. Petrus (de jonge) Vanhee was born on 9 Apr 1713 in Eernegem. He died on 17 Jan 1789 in Eernegem.

He was married to Maria Catherina Van Houtte (daughter of Petrus Van Houtte) on 18 Jul 1743 in Eernegem. Maria Catherina Van Houtte was born on 21 Jun 1703 in Torhout. She died on 15 May 1744 in Eernegem. Petrus (de jonge) Vanhee and Maria Catherina Van Houtte had the following children:

+61 i. Anna Theresia Vanhee.

He was married to Jacoba Deprez on 26 Nov 1744 in Eernegem. Jacoba Deprez died on 26 Sep 1793. Petrus (de jonge) Vanhee and Jacoba Deprez had the following children:

+62 i. Petronilla Godeliva Vanhee.
63 ii. Joannes Bernardus Vanhee was born on 18 Jun 1748 in Eernegem. He died on 7 Nov 1778.
64 iii. Joanna Clara Vanhee was born on 11 Jul 1750 in Eernegem. She died on 19 Aug 1750.
+65 iv. Catherina Angelica Vanhee.
+66 v. Godeliva Joanna Vanhee.
